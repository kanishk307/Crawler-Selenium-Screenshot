April 6: Summer B Online / Revised Dates for Summer A, B and C

Provost and Senior Academic Affairs VP Joe Glover and Senior VP and COO Charlie Lane

UF will continue online delivery of coursework during the Summer B term, as has been announced for the Summer A and Summer C  terms.

The Summer B term is being shifted to start one week later than had been previously announced in the academic calendar. This shift will also affect some Summer C dates as indicated below. Summer B Classes will begin on July 6 instead of on June 29.

Other important dates to note:

  1. Advance Registration will continue through July 1
  2. Regular Registration for Summer B will be July 2
  3. Classes Begin July 6
  4. Drop/Add July 6-7
  5. Late Registration July 6-7
  6. Non-degree Registration July 6-7
  7. Withdrawal from all Summer B courses with No Fee Liability July 7
  8. Degree Applications for Summer B and Summer C: July 29
  9. S/U Grade Option July 15
  10. Withdrawal with 25% Refund July 15
  11. Fee Payments July 17
  12. Residency Reclassifications July 17
  13. Honors Theses due to College Advising Offices August 5 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  14. Drop Deadline August 7 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  15. Withdrawal Deadline August 7 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  16. Faculty Course Evaluation Period August 8 â€” August 15 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  17. Drop or Add a Course After the Drop/Withdrawal Deadline August 14 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  18. Withdraw from all Summer B courses after the Drop/Withdrawal Deadline August 14 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  19. Classes End August 14 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  20. Reading Days: None
  21. Final Exams: in class
  22. Degree Status available: August 19 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  23. Final Grades available August 20 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  24. Faculty Course Evaluations Available to Instructors: August 20 (Summer C will change to this date also)
  25. This change will also alter the end of the summer C calendar. It will now end on August 14, and Summer C classes will take a two week break June 22nd through July 3rd.

We thank you for your flexibility in accommodating this shift, since we believe pushing the start date of summer B back by a week will make it easier on students and faculty as the peak Coronavirus incidence rate declines.
