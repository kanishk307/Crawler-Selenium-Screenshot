Visit Campus

Home > Admission > Visit Campus

Visiting campus is an important part of choosing the college that’s right for you. We are thrilled that you are interested in visiting Warner Pacific University and we can’t wait to show you around!

To schedule a campus visit please contact the Admission Office by calling 503-517-1020 or emailing admissions@warnerpacific.edu.

Discover why an Oregon private college is right for you during Oregon Private College Week (OPCW)!

Monday, July 27 – Friday, July 31, 2020

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 public health crisis, OPCW cannot be held in-person this year. However, OPCW 2020 is going virtual! Ten independent, nonprofit colleges will each host three, 80-minute virtual visit experiences during the 5-day span. Click here to view Warner Pacific University’s schedule and register today! 

Where to Go

Our Mt. Tabor campus is located at 2219 SE 68th Ave, Portland, OR 97215.

You’ll begin your visit in the Admission Office on the 2nd floor of Egtvedt Hall, which you’ll find directly connected to the main parking lot off of our SE Division entrance. Visitor parking is clearly marked.

Where to Stay

There are many lodging options near our campus.

As required by Title IX and 34 CFR §106.9, Warner Pacific University does not discriminate on the basis of sex in its educational programs and activities.
