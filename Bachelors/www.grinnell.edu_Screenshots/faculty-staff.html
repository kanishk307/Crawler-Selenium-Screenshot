  • Information for Faculty and Staff

What you need to know:

  • We have a contract with a private testing firm, Tempus, for initial and regularized COVID-19 testing for students, faculty, and staff who will be on campus this fall.
  • Remote work requirements (with only critical operations on campus) remain in place until further notice.  
  • The planning continues for how we return to the workplace or transition to new ways of working in order to protect faculty and staff.
  • We are planning for a low-density population on campus this fall.
  • Condensed academic terms will allow us to bring students safely onto campus in phases.
  • Anyone entering a campus building or public space must wear a face mask, shield, or other face covering and consistently practice physical distancing.
  • No Grinnell-funded travel will be permitted until further notice.
  • Grinnell athletic teams will not compete against other colleges during Fall Term 1.

Academic Year 2020-21 Planning Update

The next academic year will consist of four 7.5-week terms, with an optional summer term. We are planning for a low-density campus this fall, with a minimum starting number of between 500 - 600 students for the first term. There will be in-person and distance class offerings each term.
