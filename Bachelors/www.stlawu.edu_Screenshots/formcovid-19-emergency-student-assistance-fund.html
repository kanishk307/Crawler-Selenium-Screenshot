COVID-19 Emergency Student Assistance Fund

St. Lawrence University, through generous donations from members of the Laurentian Community, has established the Emergency Student Assistance Fund to support the unexpected costs due to the extraordinary circumstances of the global COVID-19 outbreak. Current students will be able to apply for these donated funds.

We recognize that the current situation may have caused unanticipated financial challenges for our undergraduate students. Please know that we care. It is expected that the student needing financial assistance will have first exhausted their immediate support network (i.e., family, friends). This fund will ensure the availability of resources for a greater number of students who lack the financial means to cover unexpected emergency expenses due to COVID-19.

Examples of How the Coronavirus Emergency Student Assistance Fund Will Be Used

  • expenses related to student emergency travel (i.e. study abroad or travel assistance off campus)
  • medical fees
  • new technology for online classes
  • temporary housing and supplies
  • storage and moving

If you are in need of financial assistance, please complete and submit the form below.
