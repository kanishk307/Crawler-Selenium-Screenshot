    For over 100 years, Massachusetts Maritime Academy has been preparing women and men for exciting and rewarding careers on land and sea. As the nation's finest co-ed maritime college, MMA challenges students to succeed by balancing a unique regimented lifestyle with a typical four-year college environment. As a member of the cadet corps you will live, study, sail, work and play in an atmosphere that encourages you to be your best.

COVID-19: Updates, Resources and Information

As you have been informed by the President's Office, we will be suspending academic instruction and campus access for the week beginning Monday, March 16. On March 23rd we will move to a predonimantly online format. 

Cadets are directed to vacate the residence halls (including the dormitory, Mariner Inn and Hampton Inn) over the coming weekend but not later than 1200 on Tuesday 17 March. Cadets who have a hardship vacating the residence halls may request permission to remain in the dorms. Space will also be made available for cadets in need of storage for their gear. Information on this process can be found at http://bit.ly/MMAHousingPetition or by visiting the Commandant's office.
