Virtual Coaching on Health and Wellness

Feel free to contact Katie Bean, director of prevention and advocacy, to schedule a virtual coaching session if you need support maintaining healthy habits while navigating this new reality.

Health and wellness topics can include:

  • stress management
  • time management
  • relationships
  • alcohol/drug use
  • nutrition
  • exercise and sleep
  • all areas of the wellness wheel

Please note that these sessions are not considered counseling or therapy. Email Katie Bean at to schedule a session or visit our website for further details.
