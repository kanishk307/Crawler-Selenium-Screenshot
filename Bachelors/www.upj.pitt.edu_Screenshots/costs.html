Costs

The University of Pittsburgh at Johnstown makes every effort to offer you an affordable college education.

  • Check our net price calculator.
  • Book prices can be found here.

Please note that more than 85% of our students receive financial assistance, including grants, scholarships, loans, and work-study employment, totaling to more than $36 million.

Information related to the Financial Responsibility Agreement can be found here.
