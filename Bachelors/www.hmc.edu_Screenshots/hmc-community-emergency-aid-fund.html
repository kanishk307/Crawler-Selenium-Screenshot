HMC Community Emergency Aid Fund

We have heard from many alumni, parents, trustees and friends asking how they can help the College and its students, faculty and staff during these unprecedented times. In response to the increasing, unforeseen demands on our community, we have created the Community Emergency Aid Fund to provide immediate support to address the operational changes needed to effectively respond to the COVID-19 global pandemic. This includes, but is not limited to, salary support and financial aid as well as technology and supplies needed for classroom learning on- or off-campus during the 2019–2020 and 2020–2021 academic years.

If you would like to support the College through this fund, you can make a donation online. If you have questions or if you need assistance making your gift, please email development@hmc.edu.

We thank you for your concern and support during these challenging times.

Stay safe. Stay at home. Stay healthy.
