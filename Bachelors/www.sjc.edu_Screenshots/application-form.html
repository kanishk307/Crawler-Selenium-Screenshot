Careers After St. John's

We know that many students have been financially impacted by COVID-19. As a result, the college issued room refunds, board refunds, and continued work study to eligible students in order to defray student costs for the spring semester.

Going forward, St. John’s has received limited federal funding under the CARES Act that may be distributed to students for “expenses related to the disruption of campus operations due to coronavirus including eligible expenses under a student’s cost of attendance such as food, housing, course materials, technology, health care, and child care.”

If you have been affected financially by the disruption of campus operations, please complete the form below. Note that the funding under the CARES Act is limited to U.S. citizens and permanent residents who have completed a FAFSA for the 2019–20 academic year. Also, these funds cannot be used to address changes in income for you or your parents. If you or your parents have experienced significant changes in income due to COVID-19 and are concerned about costs for the 2020–21 academic year, please contact the Financial Aid Office.
