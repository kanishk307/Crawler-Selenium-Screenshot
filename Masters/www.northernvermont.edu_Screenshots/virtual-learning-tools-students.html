Visit the NVU OIT website for installation instructions.

OneDrive,a cloud storage app, is also available for free toVSC students. Learn how to install OneDrive at the NVU OIT website.

Discounts on Internet Access at Home

Some Internet Service Providers (ISPs) are currently providing free or discounted Internet services to households for a limited period of time. Some ISPs may have requirements for free or discounted access.

  • Comcast -https://www.internetessentials.com/
  • Consolidated Communications - Call: 1-855-399-3084, mention the promo code "Two Months Free"
  • Spectrum -https://www.spectrum.com/browse/content/spectrum-internet-assist

Northern Vermont Universityis not affiliated with and does not endorse any individual ISP. Anyone taking advantage of free or discounted services should carefully read the ISP's terms of agreement.
