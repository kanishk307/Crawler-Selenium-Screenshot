Campus Updates

June 30 Pay Warrant Distribution
Wednesday, June 24, 2020 - 9:02am

TO: Sonoma State Employees 
FR: Payroll and Benefits
RE: June 30 live pay warrant distribution 

The upcoming payday for faculty and staff is Tuesday, June 30. 

Plan for Phased Staffing Announced
Tuesday, June 16, 2020 - 5:48pm

Sonoma State will gradually bring back employees to work on campus beginning July 6. Employees who are 65 or older or COVID-vulnerable will continue to telecommute or use the appropriate administrative leave. Employees who can effectively work remotely are encouraged to continue to telecommute for the remainder of the Fall 2020 semester.

June 15 live pay warrant distribution
Thursday, June 4, 2020 - 9:11am

Details for hourly, intermittent and student employees regarding distribution of the upcoming June 15 pay warrant.

Message from the President: Fall 2020 Update
Wednesday, June 3, 2020 - 4:09pm

President Judy K. Sakaki provides an update on what the Fall 2020 semester will be like at Sonoma State.

Direct Deposit Statements Suspended
Wednesday, May 20, 2020 - 5:03pm

In response to COVID-19, the State Controller’s Office is temporarily suspending the printing of direct deposit advice statements effective May 20, 2020. This change will remain in place through the Governor’s State of Emergency.

President Announces Extension of Temporary Telecommuting
Tuesday, May 19, 2020 - 8:52am

Because of the continued state and local orders related to COVID-19, Sonoma State University is extending our temporary telecommuting program. The earliest that eligible employees will return to work on campus will be July 6, 2020, after which we anticipate using a phased approach to resuming on-campus operations.

Temporary Processing Changes Extended
Monday, May 18, 2020 - 4:34pm

Processing changes that were put into effect earlier this semester in response to the COVID-19 pandemic have been extended. This processes include travel, purchasing and university equipment.

President's Message Regarding Chancellor's Statement on Fall 2020
Tuesday, May 12, 2020 - 7:01pm

In response to Chancellor Timothy White's announcement that all 23 campuses of the CSU system will go to remote learning for fall 2020, SSU President Judy K. Sakaki provides her response to the campus community.

Reminder Regarding May 15 Pay Warrant Distribution
Tuesday, May 12, 2020 - 12:01pm

Detailed information is provided for the hourly, intermittent and student employee pay warrant distribution on Friday, May 15.

Phishing: Criminals stealing direct deposit payments
Wednesday, May 6, 2020 - 4:40pm

With the upcoming disbursement of funds from the CARES Act, we wish to remind all users to be extra vigilant to avoid falling for phishing email scams. Higher education institutions have been seeing criminals steal direct deposit payments by tricking users into revealing passwords, then logging in to personal accounts and changing direct deposit information.
