Physical Safety

Cleaning and Disinfection

The cleanliness and disinfection of campus facilities is at the forefront of the campus response to COVID-19 and our efforts to bring employees and students back to campus safely. To this end, the university is restricting building access to limited areas to ensure that there are adequate supplies, equipment and staff available for cleaning and disinfection. Facilities Management has trained staff and procured additional cleaning supplies, including equipment that sanitizes large areas using sprayers and foggers.

Classes and administrative activities are being carefully scheduled to ensure that spaces can be sanitized between activities. Additionally, all occupied buildings on campus will be cleaned at least once daily, with additional sanitation of common-touch surfaces throughout the day.  

Face Covers and Social Distancing

Everyone is required to wear a face cover while in SSU buildings. Face coverings are also required in outdoor community spaces where physical distancing is not possible.

Wellness screening

Beginning July 13, employees who are approved to return to on-campus work on a full-time or part-time basis are required to complete a daily employee wellness check. The Qualtrics-based application requires LDAP sign-on as verification. All employees will receive instructions on the access and use of the screening application and will only be required to complete the survey on the days they come to campus.

Student wellness protocols are in development in anticipation of students returning to campus in August.

Personal Hygiene and Hand Sanitizer

Hand sanitizer stations are placed strategically throughout all open buildings. Custodial staff is responsible for placing, maintaining and refilling hand sanitizer dispensing stations in areas they serve. Additionally, the university has a limited supply of hand sanitizer and disinfecting wipes that will be provided to employee office suites and common areas. For access to more supplies and to report services needed at hand sanitizer dispensing stations, please place a work order through Facilities Management.
