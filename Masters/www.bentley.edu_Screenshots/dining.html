On-Campus Dining

Food at the 921 and other on-campus locations will be served in disposable containers, and students, faculty and staff will be able to sit in most dining locations. We anticipate that all of our dining locations including late night at Harry’s (no alcohol served at the present time due to current guidelines), Lower Café, Einstein’s, The Nest and Currito’s will be open. To promote distancing, the dining locations will have fewer seats, and seating may be created in other locations throughout campus to accommodate overflow. Dining locations will look different with directional arrows and floor markings reinforcing distancing. Touchless entry will be added to the 921.

All employees of Sodexo, our food service provider, are required to follow the company's COVID-19 safety measures, which include wearing face coverings and self-monitoring for symptoms.

Grubhub on Campus

The Grubhub food ordering app will be available for students, faculty and staff to pre-order meals at on-campus dining locations with the exception of Dunkin’, who have their own app. Grubhub will be loaded with students’ meal plan details, and students can spend their Falcon Funds and discretionary funds on campus using the Grubhub app. It will allow students to place a pre-order in whatever dining location they choose, so the food is made to order and ready for pick-up when they arrive. No tipping will be required when Grubhub is used.

Faculty and Staff Dining Room

The Faculty and Staff Dining Room is planning to be open, and seating will be limited to allow for appropriate distancing.
