Coronavirus Information

Excelsior College remains committed to helping our students stay on track with their educational plans during the coronavirus (COVID-19) public health crisis. We continue to monitor the COVID-19 impact upon our students, faculty, and staff and will keep you updated as events unfold.

Tried-and-true tips to help you stay on track with your studies.

Juggling work, life, and school demands is even more challenging because of COVID-19.  Try these tips to help yourself successfully manage your studies:

  • Time management tips for students
  • Procrastination and ways to avoid it
  • Study plans – their importance and how to use them


Virtual Commencement Planned

The events surrounding COVID-19 have raised insurmountable concerns with protecting the health and safety of our graduates and their families, faculty, and staff for a traditional Commencement. As it is still important to all of us to honor the day and based on feedback, we will celebrate on July 10 with a virtual Commencement ceremony. More details will follow soon and you can find up-to-date information on our Commencement webpage.

We also encourage you to stay informed about COVID-19 and take steps to prevent the spread of all respiratory illnesses. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) provides helpful information:

  • Prevention and protecting yourself
  • General information about COVID-19
  • Frequently asked questions about COVID-19
  • Travel Notices
  • Manage Anxiety and Stress
