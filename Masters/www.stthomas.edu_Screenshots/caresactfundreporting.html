CARES Act Fund Reporting

The Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security (CARES) Act signed into law March 28, 2020, provides almost $14 billion that will go directly to higher education institutions to support the costs of shifting classes online, grants to students for food, housing, technology, and other purposes.

St. Thomas has received $2.5 million from the CARES Act for student aid and we have focused on granting the money to students in both our undergraduate and graduate programs, including incoming students.

You can find more detail in this 30-day report on St. Thomas CARES Funding

Current and incoming students can apply for CARES Act Emergency Grants by following the instructions on One St. Thomas (the school intranet)
