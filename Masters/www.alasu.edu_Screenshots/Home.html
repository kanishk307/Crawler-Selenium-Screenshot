Coronavirus (COVID 19) Updates

LATEST UPDATES FROM THE UNIVERSITY

 

Greetings ASU Students:

We are excited to report that the University has issued emergency financial aid grants as part of the CARES Act to all of those students who were initially identified as eligible to receive the payments under the guidelines established by the federal government. That means that the large majority of our students have already received their payments. We hope that these funds will be of assistance to you as you face whatever challenges that may have been presented due to the Coronavirus pandemic.

For those students who have not received a CARES emergency financial aid grant payment from ASU and you feel that you are eligible, please visit www.alasu.edu/caresgrantform and complete the form that you will find there. Please follow the instructions as given and allow time for your submission to be processed. We will work diligently to avoid any delays in notifying you whether or not you are eligible and to process payments for those who are to receive them.

Please know that the University is committed to distributing the funds to every student who meets the federal requirements as quickly as possible.

Stay safe!

Sincerely,

William F. Hopper

Interim Vice President for Business and Finance
