Residence Hall Spring 2020 Check-Out

Next

Northern  students currently not residing on campus can move out of their rooms starting Friday, March 27, through Monday, April 6. Starting at noon on Wednesday, March 25, students will need to contact the Office of Residence Life at 605-626-3007 or 605-626-3008 to schedule a three-hour window (9 a.m.-noon; noon-3 p.m.; 3-6 p.m.) in which they will be permitted to return to campus to complete their check-out.  Strict adherence to each student’s scheduled time will allow the check-out to proceed within the social distancing criteria that will support the safety of everyone involved. Compliance with the indicated check-out times will also allow Northern to adequately staff each check-out site. 

Students currently residing on campus may begin the express check-out beginning Thursday morning. 

If a student needs to continue living on campus past the April 6, 2020, date, they must complete a Request for Accommodation form by Friday, April 3, 2020. 

The bookstore will be open from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. March 27-April 6 (all days) so students can pick up mail and return rentals before they leave. Students have until May 29 to get their rentals back. 

General Conditions

  1. No additional residents will be allowed to occupy any residence hall space beyond those students already occupying a residence hall space. 
  1. Should a student live outside of a 5-hour radius of the campus, they will be permitted to return to campus the evening before their scheduled departure time. Those students will need to schedule for a morning check-out.
  1. Only students with scheduled check-out times will be permitted into the halls. Students will not be permitted to come into halls to pick up random individual items.
  1. Students may not have friends or family complete their check-out without securing prior permission from the Director of Residence Life.
  1. Students must take all belongings with them at the time of their departure. They are not permitted to leave belongings in their current room, even if they plan to occupy that room in the fall. 
  1. Students can be accompanied by only one person in addition to themselves to complete their checkout.
  1. All perimeter doors will be locked and should not be propped open during the move-out process. Key cards will work only during the assigned time so please be prompt.
  1. This is intended to be an “Express Check-Out” experience; thus, after you are done moving out, simply return your keys to the designated space in the lobby/desk area on the ground floor.
  1. Refunds will be provided according to the Refund Procedure, which is detailed on the NSU COVID-19 webpage.
  1. If you are unable to attend this initial window for check-out, a “Second Chance Check-Out” will be held between May 1 and May 9. Details for this will be provided in a follow-up posting.
  1. Items left in the students’ rooms after May 9 will be packed and stored by the university at the owner’s expense.
  1. During the check-out, on-site Residential Life personnel reserve the right to regulate traffic to a floor to maintain appropriate social distancing.
  1. Students should give consideration to other campus obligations they may have by contacting those departments prior to their return to check-out of their room. Things to consider: returning books to the library; returning athletic equipment or uniforms; returning any other materials or supplies associated with an academic department.
