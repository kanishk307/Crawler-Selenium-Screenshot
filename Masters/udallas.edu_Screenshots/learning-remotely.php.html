Access UD Official Email

All official communication will be directed through your University of Dallas email.

  • Verify you can log in to your UD Email (My UD )
  • If you have problems logging in, contact support@udallas.edu

Minimum Technology Suggestions

Your technology requirements may vary based on the instructor or course, review your syllabus to see if these requirements are listed.

Examples of technology requirements could include:

  • High speed internet
  • Word processing program
  • Email
  • Webcam
  • Microphone/Speaker or Headset

System Check

Communication

Your individual professors should be contacting you with a plan for completing the course.  If you have questions, reach out to the contact information listed on your syllabus.
