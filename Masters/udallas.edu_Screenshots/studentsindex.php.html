University Going Online For the Rest of the Semester

Dear Students,

I understand President Hibbs just sent an email regarding the university going online for the rest of the semester. I understand how jarring and heartbreaking this must be, especially for the senior class. Please know we are here for you and want to support you anyway we can.

The Office of Student Affairs is establishing a process for students with belongings in the residence halls and student apartments to gather your belongings and move out. More details will be shared as soon as possible. If you have already scheduled a time this weekend to gather your belongings, please plan on taking everything with you. You will have as much time as you need on your scheduled day to retrieve your belongings. If you have not scheduled a time to gather your belongings, please wait for further communication before coming back to campus to retrieve your possessions.

Again, please know we support you and are praying for you and your families.

Take care,
Seth Oldham
Director of Student Affairs

President Hibbs Announces Early Start and Early End to Fall 2020 Semester
6/9/2020
Classes will now begin on August 19, one week earlier than the previously scheduled start date of August 26. Finals week will now end the day before Thanksgiving, on November 25.

Fall 2020 Preparations
6/8/2020
In order to take advantage of the current diminishment in the incidence rate of infection by COVID-19 and to prevent the challenge of students returning after Thanksgiving from all parts of the U.S., I have decided to change the fall semester.

Return to On-Campus Work
5/22/2020
Last week, an Employee Pulse Survey was sent out to all faculty and staff to measure your level of comfort in returning to work on campus. Survey results showed a near-even split.
