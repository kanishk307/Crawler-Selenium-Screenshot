Financial Aid and Tuition During COVID-19

As we continue to move forward during the COVID-19 pandemic, we want to remind students that we are here to support you and that financial aid and emergency funds are available.

Financial Aid

  • See the Financial Aid website for the most current information about changes and options for financial aid for Spring and Summer 2020 and the 2020–21 school year, including CARES Act funding and emergency grants.
  • See the Financial Services website for information about receiving funds during remote operations.

Tuition and Fees

As Evergreen has shifted to remote instruction, we have increased our investment in tools, training, and technology to support student and faculty success in this form of instruction. At the same time, we are making existing and new services available online and investing in new technological capabilities to serve students remotely, sustain critical functions, and support the Evergreen community.

Additionally, state law sets residency policy and rules for determining who is charged out-of-state tuition.

The following fees are not currently being charged:

  • Children’s Center
  • Parking
  • Late Night Transit (not typically charged for Summer quarter)

Summer 2020

Summer 2020 tuition rates are similar to the prior Fall, Winter and Spring quarters with a flat rate from 10 credits up to 18 credits. Rates per credit were also lowered by a average of 3%. New student, Graduation, and Wellness fees will not be charged, as is usual for Summer quarter. See Summer 2020 tuition tables for details.

Fall 2020

Tuition rates are set by the Washington state legislature and are updated annually, usually in June.
