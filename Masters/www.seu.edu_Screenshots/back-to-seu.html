COVID-19 Recovery Team Mini-Series

We’re excited for the return of our students to campus this fall!

The COVID-19 Recovery Team (CRT) has been working diligently to ensure that every possible aspect of campus life is continuously evaluated, and the best possible health and safety measures are either added or improved upon. We have created a video mini-series to show you what we’re doing to prepare for your arrival to campus. We’ll be sure to update these videos as needed as our preparations evolve, so be sure to check back!
