    Leadership

    Established in 1851, we are the third oldest college in California. NDNU is a Catholic, not-for-profit, coeducational university, located on the San Francisco peninsula in Silicon Valley. With more than 1,500 students from 32 states and 28 different countries, NDNU exposes students to broad interests and perspectives through others with diverse backgrounds.

    Notre Dame de Namur's intimate environment supports a well-rounded experience that is hard to find at large universities.
