Reopen Status for Campus Offices

 

Current Offices Status Spreadsheet

 

Based on State Orders and guidance from public health officials regarding COVID-19, Southeastern moved instruction and most campus services and activities to remote operations in mid-March. Over recent weeks, the State has made significant progress in addressing this public health emergency, and consistent with Federal and State guidelines on phased reopening, the University has been implementing a phased incremental return of campus operations.  

As a result, a number of University offices are at least partially reopened with others expected to resume on-campus operations in the weeks ahead. This spreadsheet provides an updated list of offices and departments that have resumed some level of campus presence, along with their relevant status. Please keep in mind that all units continue to provide services by telephone and email, irrespective of campus operational status.

Campus personnel should send any corrections or updates to carolyn.lefebvre@southeastern.edu.
