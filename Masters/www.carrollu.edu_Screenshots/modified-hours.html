Carroll University Modified Hours of Operation

Coronavirus (COVID-19) Update

To comply with the recommendations of public health authorities and ensure the health and safety of our campus, some of our facilities may be closed and some offices may have limited hours for in-person service. We have increased our hand sanitizer/hand wipe stations in those facilities and offices that are open to the public.

Unless otherwise noted, all university offices are available via email or phone Monday through Friday 8 – 4:30 p.m.
