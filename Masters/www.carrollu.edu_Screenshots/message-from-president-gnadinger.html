A Message From President Gnadinger

May 20, 2020

Dear Students, Faculty, Staff and Parents,

I hope this message finds everyone safe and well. Over the past several weeks, we’ve received many questions in regard to the upcoming fall semester at Carroll. I understand that everyone is anxiously awaiting news. With the health and safety of our campus community as our top priority, we are considering several options.

As we’ve learned in this situation, flexibility is key in being able to quickly pivot in response to public health guidelines. There are complex challenges we need to address, and unknowns that make planning more challenging. With that in mind, I’ve convened a Fall 2020 Planning Task Force, made up of faculty and staff leadership representing all areas of campus. They have been meeting regularly to explore a variety of scenarios as we prepare for a “new normal”.

This short video message discusses their work and some of the considerations we’re taking into account  as we make decisions about the fall semester at Carroll. I’ll look forward to sharing additional details at our Town Hall meetings in the coming weeks.

Sincerely,

Cindy Gnadinger
President
