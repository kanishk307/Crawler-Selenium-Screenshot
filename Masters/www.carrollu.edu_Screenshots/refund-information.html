Carroll University COVID-19 Refund Information

Coronavirus (COVID-19) Update | Last updated June 3, 2020

Carroll University responded to the abrupt departure of residential students following spring break by offering housing and meal plan credits. A brief summary of our refunding plan is noted below. More details about housing and meal plan credits, plus answers to other FAQs related to COVID-19 refunds can be found here.

Housing and Meal Plans

  • A 50 percent credit will be given to currently registered students for the Spring 2020 Semester housing and meal plan charges.
  • Refunds for housing and meal plans will be adjusted based on financial aid regulations if appropriate.
  • Credits will first be applied to any outstanding student account balances.
  • Graduating seniors will receive a refund check for any remaining credit balance.
  • Credit balances remaining for all other students will be applied to the next academic term.
  • There will be no housing or meal plan refund for those students choosing to remain on campus.

Registration

Registration holds for current students were removed through May 4 to allow students to register for summer and fall terms.

Outstanding Balances

  • Late fees incurred through June will be waived.
  • If you have an outstanding balance and need to adjust your payment plan, or would like to set up a payment plan, please contact the business office at busoff@carrollu.edu.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find more details about housing and meal plan credits, plus answers to other FAQs related to COVID-19 refunds.

If you have additional questions, please contact us at COVID-19refunds@carrollu.edu.
