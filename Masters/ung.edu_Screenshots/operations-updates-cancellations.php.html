        Academic Advising Operations Update

        During this time of remote instruction, all advising appointments with professional advisors in campus Advising Centers will continue to take place virtually using Office 365 Teams. 

        Students who have not yet made appointments can still book them with professional advisors through adviseme.ung.edu. Your advisor will send you simple instructions for joining the advising meeting through Teams once your appointment is confirmed. 

        If you need additional accommodations, please email your advisor through your UNG email account.

        Library Operations Updates

        Your Library, Online

        Library staff are working remotely as our physical locations (except the Dahlonega library) remain closed. Online help is available during the following hours:

        • Monday - Thursday 8:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m.
        • Friday 8:00 a.m. - 3:00 p.m.
        • Sunday 2:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m.

        *Please check the library website for the latest updates in service hours.

        All contact methods (chat, email, voicemail, and webforms) are monitored during these hours and responded to as quickly as possible. Our Cumming, Dahlonega and Gainesville locations are open to UNG affiliates only from 10:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. Monday - Thursday.

        Reference and Informational Questions - Please use chat or email  so that we may route your question to the appropriate person. 

        Research Consultations - Requests for consultations are being delivered remotely (e.g. Teams, Skype, Blackboard Collaborate.) Please allow 48 business hours advance notice from the time of your request in order to allow for adequate preparation.

        Library Instruction - Sessions are being delivered remotely (e.g. Teams, Skype, Blackboard Collaborate) or instructors may request instructional materials for inclusion in D2L.  Any instruction that has not already been scheduled, should be requested at least two weeks in advance to allow for adequate preparation. 

        Electronic Resources are available 24/7. For assistance use chat or email on the Libraries website.

        Physical Items are available for checkout in the Dahlonega Library during open hours. Due dates for all materials have been extended and items (books, technology) should not be returned until facilities reopen. For questions please use email.

        Gil Express is currently unavailable. Items currently checked out should be held until facilities reopen.

        Interlibrary Loan (ILL) is currently for borrowing of digital items only. Items currently checked out should be held until facilities reopen.
