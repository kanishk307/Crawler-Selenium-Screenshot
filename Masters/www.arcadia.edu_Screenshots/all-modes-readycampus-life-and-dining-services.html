Fall 2020 Campus Life and Dining Services

Campus Life

Staying connected outside of the classroom is essential to your Arcadia experience. We are designing new opportunities for you to engage and socialize virtually and in person. Regardless of where you are, we are committed to helping you get involved.

More information will be shared regarding student clubs, organizations, and activities as we approach the fall semester. 

Dining Services

Dining facilities are prepared to offer meals starting on Aug. 17. A fourth dining facility on campus, a new eatery/coffee shop in the Oak Summit lobby, will offer a variety of options including Starbucks coffee. Metz Dining Services will have to-go options for students, and dining facilities will be set up to allow for social distancing while eating.
