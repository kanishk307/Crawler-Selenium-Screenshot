Office Operations

Most Trinity offices have transitioned to working remotely. Find basic operational overviews for each Trinity area below. Use the A-Z service guide for specific Trinity operations.

Coates Library

Available Remote Services: Many of the Library's holdings can be accessed online. Trinity faculty, staff, and students may, however, request physical items for retrieval and pick-up at designated times. More information on how to check out physical items >>

Hours of Operation: Users can pick up library books in the lobby two days a week: Tuesdays and Thursdays, at 10-10:30 a.m., 12:30-1 p.m., and 3-3:30 p.m.

Facilities Services

Available Remote Services: The Facilities Service request center is still receiving requests.

Hours of Operation: Staff from Facilities Services will be on campus normal business hours Monday through Friday.

Preferred Contact Method: The on campus staff will respond to all calls and will then call in the specific trade as necessary to complete the task. After hours calls and emergency requests will continue to go to TUPD (210-999-7070). To place a work request follow one of these simple steps.

  1. Voice call 210-999-8413

  2. Send an Email to Service Request (service-request@trinity.edu)

  3. Online self reporting

Human Resources

Available Remote Services: Human Resources continues to provide all services throughout this period of reduced operations.

Hours of Operation: The department continues to only staff the office in Northrup Hall on M/W/F. All HR employees continue to work remotely, and are responding to emails and phone calls as per normal operating procedures.

Preferred Contact Method: Please feel free to contact Barbara Centeno, chief human resources officer, at 210-999-7551 if you have any questions or are having difficulty reaching any of the staff.

Additional Notes: All forms you may need to submit should be scanned or saved as a pdf and sent to humanresources@trinity.edu. If you do not have a scanner, please take a picture of the form and send it to the same email address. Thank you for your support during this period.

Ricoh Digital Print Center

Available Remote Services: The Ricoh Digital Print Center in the Coates Library is still available to serve your printing needs.

Preferred Contact Method: Requests for printing service should be sent to dpp@trinity.edu

Additional Notes: Please see instructions for submitting Print Shop Orders below in the A-Z Service Guide.

A-Z Service Guide

If you do not see a service listed, please use our Online Directory to contact the office that can assist you.

B

Budget Transfer Form

How to submit a budget transfer:
Complete the budget request form from the Business Office electronically and attach appropriate support for the request. A single pdf (including form and backup) should be emailed to budget@trinity.edu.

C

Cash Transmittal Form

Used to submit cash or checks to Student Financial Services.

  • Student Financial Services has reduced their hours. Cash or checks may still be dropped off Monday through Friday 10am-2pm.

Contracts

As a reminder, any modifications or addendums to existing contracts need to be reviewed by Risk Management.

COVID-19 Related Institutional Expenses

Please fill out this form if you have an institutional expense related to COVID-19 for potential reimbursement by the federal government. In addition to this form, please route the appropriate expense as usual via Accounts Payable (payment net if a pcard was used or invoice/PO submission to AP with the account number and appropriate signatures).

D

Direct Deposit

All employees are encouraged to set up direct deposit. Instructions here.

E

Equipment Transaction Form (006)

Used to request transfer of an asset (including equipment or furniture), or to report the sale or theft of a university-owned asset.

F

Form W-4

Used to make changes to federal income tax withholding from wages.

  • Complete the form electronically and email to payroll@trinity.edu

  • Only include the last four digits of your social security number on the form.

I

Invoices

Payment request received from vendors.

  • Instead of using the physical invoice stamp, include the following details in the body of your email:

    • PO/BPO #

    • Account number(s)

    • Amount

    • Approval(s)

  • All invoices should be routed for approval based on existing approval levels. Indicate approval(s) in the email. Departments are responsible for routing the invoice for second approval when the amount is $5,000 or more before submitting to Accounts Payable for payment.

  • An alternative to providing payment details in the email message is to add a text box on the invoice that includes the amount to be charged to each general ledger account and name of the person approving the payment. We recommend using the Digital Signature feature within Adobe.

  • Please send pdf to accountspayable@trinity.edu.

  • Payment by p-card is highly recommended for small dollar purchases/invoices. Accounts Payable will indicate when an invoice is coming from a p-card preferred vendor. If you donâ€™t have a p-card to make the payment, note this when the approved invoice is sent to Accounts Payable.

J

Journal Entry Form

Use the Journal Entry Form to request general ledger corrections or transfers of funding.

  • Complete the form electronically and attach appropriate support for the request. A single pdf (including form and backup) should be emailed to bvallejo@trinity.edu.

M

Mail Processing

  • All mail and packages will be held for 48 hours prior to sorting. All mail will be sorted and packages scanned into our system.

  • The warehouse and mail center employees are rotating days in central receiving. Packages can be picked up any day, but mail can only be picked up Monday, Wednesday, and Friday while the mail center staff is on site.

  • Please call central receiving (210-999-8291) or the mail center (210-999-7220) to verify you have mail or a package ready for pickup.

  • To pick up either mail or packages, arrive at the loading dock door (Prassel Garage next to the tennis courts), ring the bell, and when prompted announce who you are and what department(s) you are picking up for.

  • In order to create social distance, after staff has placed your items close to the edge and has moved away from the door, they will raise the door. Please gather your mail and the door will close.

  • If your outgoing mail is stamped and ready to be sent, please place it in the U.S. post office blue box outside the dock doors.

  • Any outgoing mail that needs postage added should be dropped off at the loading dock door when you pick up your mail to prevent multiple trips. Please ensure a staff member retrieves your mail before leaving the loading dock area.

Mileage Reimbursement Request

Use this form when requesting reimbursement for local business mileage, not including mileage as part of an approved conference or other business trip that includes meals, hotels, or other expenses.

  • Complete the form electronically, route for approval, and forward to accountspayable@trinity.edu.

P

Payment Request Form

Used to request payment when there is not an invoice. Most commonly used for business expense reimbursements.

  • Complete and submit with approvals to accountspayable@trinity.edu

  • Either scan or take pictures of your receipts and submit them with the form.

  • Expenses should continue to be substantiated with details for the business purpose included on the form.

Petty Cash Requests

We are no longer accepting requests for petty cash. Please use a p-card for minor purchases, or keep your receipts and submit a Payment Request Form to Accounts Payable.

Print Shop Orders

Requests for printing service should be sent to dpp@trinity.edu with a pdf of the file. Please include instructions such as single or double sided, black/white or color, type of finish if applicable, quantity, and the department number if it is a department charge. If you are accustomed to using the TRAC program for job requests, the program does not work in an off-campus setting. Working remotely requires using the email address above.

For personal jobs, payment should be made with a Tiger Card. The job total will be provided and charged to your card by providing us with your ID number. There may be a delay in posting the charge to your account.

Once the job is complete, it will be shipped to you at the address you provided. Please note, shipping costs will apply.

Purchasing Card Envelopes

Retain all p-card receipts until further notice. This includes receipts and p-card statements for the month of February and onward until normal operations resume.

R

Requisition Form

Used to make purchases totaling $5,000 or more.

  • Complete the form electronically, and route for approvals. Approved form and supporting documentation (quote, signed contracts, etc) should be emailed to accountspayble@trinity.edu.

T

Travel Expense Report for Non-Concur Travel

Use this excel report to list, reconcile, and request reimbursement for travel expenses.

  • Complete the form electronically and attach all substantiating documentation, such as the original receipts for which reimbursement is being requested. Route for approval, and return to accountspayable@trinity.edu.

  • Either scan or take pictures of your receipts and submit them with your report.

V

Vendor Checks and Reimbursements

Vendor payments will be mailed to the remittance address on file. The most critical payments will be handled with priority. This includes payments to students, utilities, governmental and regulatory agencies, insurance, and major contractors. We are not able to accommodate vendors who wish to pick up checks in person.
