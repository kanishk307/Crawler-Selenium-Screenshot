    Important Status Update Regarding ASI Events

    Date: 3/12/2020
    From: Associated Students Incorporated 
    Subject: Important Status Update Regarding ASI Events

    Dear Broncos,

    Per the University, “all events and gatherings hosted by Cal Poly Pomona on or off campus with more than 100 participants are to be cancelled. Consistent with California Department of Public Health recommendations, we are promoting social distancing by canceling all events effective March 12 through May 31. When possible, events may be rescheduled, or modified using technology.”

    The safety of our Broncos, their families, and friends are our number one priority, and while the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health has NOT identified a risk of COVID-19 (coronavirus) to the Cal Poly Pomona campus, we want to support our University and the precautions around reducing close person-to-person contact.

    To view the full list of ASI events with updates regarding their status, please click here.

    We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate your understanding of the changing nature of the situation. 

    If you have any questions regarding ASI events, please email Cathy Neale, Associate Director of Marketing at caneale@cpp.edu.

    For all other campus updates, please visit CPP Safety and Emergency Information.

    Thank you,
    Associated Students Inc.
