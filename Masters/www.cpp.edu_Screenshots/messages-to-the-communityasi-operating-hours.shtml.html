    ASI Operating Hours

    Date: 3/13/2020
    From: Associated Students Incorporated 
    Subject: ASI Operating Hours

    Dear Broncos,

    The safety of our Broncos, their families, and friends are our number one priority, and while the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health has NOT identified a risk of COVID-19 (coronavirus) to the Cal Poly Pomona campus, we want to support our University and the precautions around reducing close person-to-person contact.

    ASI facilities are open for continued services to our Bronco community. The hours of operation for each facility listed below is subject to change based on the changing nature of the situation. Refer to our website at asi.cpp.edu for live hours of operations.

    Bronco Student Center (Bldg. 35)
    Monday – Friday: 7 a.m. to 10 p.m.
    Saturday: 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
    Sunday: Noon to 9 p.m.

    Spring Break hours
    Monday – Friday: 7 a.m. to 6 p.m.ASI Operating HoursSaturday – Sunday: Closed

     Note: The BSC will not be taking any new room reservations through April 3, and all existing reservations are being canceled. This is to support social distancing to reduce close person-to-person contact.

    For questions regarding the BSC, please call 909-869-2847.

    Games Room Etc.  (Bldg. 35-1110)
    Monday – Sunday: Closed until further notice
    For questions regarding the Games Room Etc., please call 909-869-4467.

    Poly Pantry  (Bldg. 35-1333)
    Monday – Friday: 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. and 4 p.m. to 6 p.m.
    Saturday and Sunday: Closed
    For questions regarding the Poly Pantry, please call 909-869-2343.

    Bronco Recreation and Intramural Complex (Bldg. 42)
    Monday – Thursday: 6 a.m. to Midnight
    Friday, 6 a.m. to 11 p.m.

    Click here for a full list of hours including the Bronco Peak and pool, which are currently open.
    Note: The BRIC is open for individual work outs and informal open recreation activities only during the building’s regular operating hours.     

    We continue to uphold our high cleaning standards and we are extra diligent about disinfecting the high touch areas around the BRIC.  We are asking all members and guest to use the equipment wipes (available in dispensers throughout the building) before and after using equipment.  Also, we encourage you to wash your hands thoroughly before and after your activity.   

    For questions regarding the BRIC, please call (909) 869-2109.

    Children’s Center
    Monday – Friday: 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. (Preschool) / 7:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. (Toddler/18 months)ASI Operating Hours.
