    Extension of Temporary Paid Administrative Leave Provisions

    Date: 4/8/2020
    From: Division of Administrative Affairs
    Subject: Extension of Temporary Paid Administrative Leave Provisions

    Dear Colleagues,

    In an effort to continue to limit the gathering of students, faculty and staff, and effective immediately, the 128 hours granted to employees for paid administrative leave has been increased to 256 hours for employees unable to work for COVID-19 related reasons. The provisions as outlined in my March 21, communication to campus remain the same. The full announcement from the CSU Office of the Chancellor can be found at HR2020-04.

    Please direct any immediate questions to Employee and Labor Relations at elr@cpp.edu or 909-869-3729.

    Thank you for all you do in support of Cal Poly Pomona’s mission.

    Danielle Manning
    Vice President for Administration, Finance, and Strategic Development and CFO
