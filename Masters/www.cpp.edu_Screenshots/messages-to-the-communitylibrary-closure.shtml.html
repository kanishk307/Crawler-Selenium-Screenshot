    Library Building and 24/7 Lab Closure

    Date: March 29, 2020
    From: University Library
    Subject: Library Building and 24/7 Lab Closure

    Dear CPP Student,

    In response to county and state orders, as well as CSU guidance calling for physical distancing to help reduce the transmission of COVID-19, the University Library building and the 24-hour computer lab will be closed until further notice.

    We have extended the due date for all checked out Library materials to May 22, 2020.

    If the closure of the 24-hour computer lab poses significant technological challenges for you, please immediately email studentsuccess@cpp.edu. You can also call the Health Alert Hotline at 909-869-2500, Monday through Friday, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. The campus has a limited number of loaner laptops available to students.

    In addition, many of the Library's resources and services continue to be available online. This includes article databases, ebooks, and 24/7 online reference chat service. Subject librarians are also available via email, phone, and scheduled online consultations. If you have any questions, please call 909-869-3075 or email libraryhelp@cpp.edu. Please visit https://libguides.library.cpp.edu/library-services-available-covid19 for links to some of these online resources.

    We are sincere in our commitment to support our students, faculty, and staff and will continue to serve you the best way possible. We apologize for the inconvenience. Visit the library website at https://www.cpp.edu/library for updates or changes.

    Stay well.

    Emma C. Gibson
    Interim Dean, University Library
