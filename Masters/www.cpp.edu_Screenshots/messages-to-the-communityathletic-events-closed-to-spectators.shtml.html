    CPP Home Athletic Events Closed To Spectators

    Date: 3/11/2020
    From: Bronco Athletics
    Subject: CPP Home Athletic Events Closed To Spectators

    Cal Poly Pomona Athletics continues to work closely with campus leadership to monitor and respond to the evolving COVID-19 situation. Based on the decisions announced today by CSU Chancellor's Office and out of an abundance of caution, attendance at all Cal Poly Pomona Athletics home events will be limited to essential personnel only until further notice. Essential personnel are defined as student-athletes, coaches, team trainers and medical personnel, game officials, operational and administrative staff, and credentialed media members.

    All of the CPP baseball home games will continue to have live stats and a live video stream, free of charge, but no spectators (fans, family members, etc.) will be allowed in or around Scolinos Field.

    Cal Poly Pomona Athletics will continue to follow California State University and Cal Poly Pomona travel guidelines when it comes to team and personnel travel. At this point in time, teams will continue to practice and compete; however, any student-athlete who does not feel comfortable traveling or participating in team activities will be excused from doing so.

    We will continue to closely monitor the situation as well as recommendations from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and the Los Angeles County Department of Public Health, and we will work together with campus officials to make any additional changes deemed in the best interest of our student-athletes, coaches, staff and fans.

    More information on the Coronavirus outbreak and preventative measures can be found here. Additional updates regarding Cal Poly Pomona Athletics events will be published here as they become available.

    In regards to CPP teams competing on the road this weekend...

    • The NCAA Division II Men's Basketball West Regional at UC San Diego in La Jolla will go on as planned, but also without the general public. Please see UCSD Athletics' information here.
    • The NCAA Division II Women's Basketball West Regional at Hawaii Pacific in Honolulu, Hawaii will also go on as planned. This story will be updated should any new developments occur.
    • The CPP men's and women's track and field teams will compete at the Ben Brown Invitational at Cal State Fullerton this weekend as planned, but no spectators will be allowed in the venue. Some members of the track and field team were also scheduled to compete at the Occidental Spring Break Classic, but that meet has been cancelled.
    • The NCAA Division II Indoor Track & Field Championships in Alabama are scheduled to go on as planned.

    This story will continue to be updated as information becomes available.
