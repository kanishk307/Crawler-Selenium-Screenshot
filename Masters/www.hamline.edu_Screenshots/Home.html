  • COVID-19 Information and Resources

    We ask that all Pipers show appropriate support for any and all members of our community who either contract the virus or who may have a loved one affected by it. Hold true to Hamline’s core value, “do all the good you can,” during this time.

  • Updates from Hamline (July 2020)

    Hamline University is open. Current operations have been adapted in response to the pandemic.

    Pipers wear masks. Hamline University has implemented a mask policy which is linked here. In summary, masks must be worn by all students and employees outdoors when unable to
    sustain at least 6 feet of physical distance from others. Masks will be worn by all students, employees and guests while indoors in common areas of campus, including hallways, classrooms and labs, offices without private office space allowing for 6 feet of physical distance and shared common areas in all campus buildings including residential facilities.

    All summer term 2020 undergraduate and graduate classes are online. 


    Graduate and Online Degree Completion Programs

    Online classes are not affected by changes to campus operations, including all graduate and undergraduate online degree completion classes that were previously delivered online.

    Graduate classes that were previously face-to-face (including residencies for hybrid programs) are being delivered online for the remainder of spring term and all of summer term.

    Prospective Students

    Prospective undergraduate students should check online for updates on Hamline’s admission process.
