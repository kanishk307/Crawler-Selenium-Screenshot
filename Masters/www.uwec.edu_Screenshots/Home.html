COVID-19 (Coronavirus) Updates

UW-Eau Claire’s Response to COVID-19

We are in close contact with the Eau Claire City-County Health Department, Barron County Health Department and other local entities regarding preparedness and response to the COVID-19 (Coronavirus) outbreak. Our goal is to assure that all our students, faculty and staff remain safe and healthy and that we continue to provide the high-quality education that defines UW-Eau Claire.

Stop the Spread: Viruses don’t discriminate

We want to remind campus that while some around the world have attached racist tropes to COVID-19, science proves that viruses don’t discriminate. The Office of Multicultural Affairs and the Equity in Student Matters Commission created a downloadable flyer with helpful tips on how to stop the spread of the virus. If you are the target of bias incident, please submit a BIRT report at the link below.

As always, our priority is your safety and we want to help you navigate through this difficult time.

  • See the downloadable and printable flyer Stop the Spread.
  • Report a bias incident to the Bias Incident Response Team at uwec.ly/birt.

More information for:

 

Prospective students and families

We have opened registration for visits starting in June. For your safety as well as the safety of our staff and campus community, our tour groups will be capped at 10 people and all attendees will be required to wear a mask. These visits are subject to change based on guidelines set forth in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. We will continue to have virtual visit options available as well.

View all visit options

Admitted students and families

All campus visits and admissions programming are canceled until further notice. However, we assure you that attending any of these events are not mandatory for your enrollment at UW-Eau Claire. We still plan to see you in the fall!

In the meantime, we encourage you to complete a few of your next steps (Transfer students, click here) and connect with Admissions on Facebook or your future classmates on the UW-Eau Claire Class of 2024 Facebook group.
