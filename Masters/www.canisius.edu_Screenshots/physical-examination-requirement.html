    PHYSICAL EXAMINATION REQUIREMENTS

    Physical Examination Requirement for New Undergraduate and Transfer Students
    (United States and Canadian Residents)

    All full time undergraduate and full time undergraduate transfer students are required to submit a completed physical examination and immunization form prior to their first class attendance. Physical examinations must be done within one year of attendance. Students playing a D1 Intercollegiate sports must have a physical done within 6 months of first sports participation.

    Download the Physical Examination and Immunization Form.

    If attending in the fall, physical forms are due in Student Health by July 31.

    If attending in the spring, physical forms are due in Student Health by January 1.

    Where to Get a Physical Examination

    Physical examinations can be obtained through your health care provider, urgent care clinics and travel clinics. For a list of clinics that provide this service in Western New York, visit myCanisiusHealth or contact Student Health at 716-888- 2610.
