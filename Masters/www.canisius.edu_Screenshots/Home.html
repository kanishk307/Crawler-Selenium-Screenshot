    Student Health

    Fostering student success through good health.

    Student Health is committed to optimizing good health.  Staffed
    by physicians and registered nurses, Student Health offers services that are easy to access, individualized and targeted to treating illnesses and injuries common to college students.  Each visit is an opportunity for students to learn how to achieve and maintain good health now and in the future.

    Welcome New Students!

    Congratulations on your acceptance to Canisius College. Student Health extends a warm welcome to you and your family. For important health information you need to know, click one of the links below:

    MyCanisiusHealth   

    MyCanisius Health is your web based health portal offering 24/7 access to Student Health and health information.  From myCanisiusHealth, you can
    •    Schedule your health center appointment
    •    Read messages from the college physicians and nurses
    •    Update your health insurance information and scan up a copy of your insurance card
    •    Review self care tips and educational materials
    •    Scan completed health forms to Student Health

    Student Privacy

    All health information received in Student Health is confidential and not released without a student’s written permission.  Student Health will not share your protected health information with anyone including faculty, coaches, family, employers, staff or friends.

    If you are under the age of 18 years, Student Health is required to share some information with parents and guardians in order to treat you.

    A full description of how Student Health protects your health information can be found on your health portal myCanisiusHealth.  Students can also request a written copy of that description.
