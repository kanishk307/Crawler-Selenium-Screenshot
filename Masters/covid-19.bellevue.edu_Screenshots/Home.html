Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) Information and Resources ›

Bellevue University is making adjustments on a daily basis during the ongoing COVID-19 disease outbreak.

If students are experiencing anxiety or need support during this time, Bellevue University provides students with free crisis counseling services in-person on the main Bellevue University campus.

Bellevue University also provides the free TalkCampus app for all local and distance students.TalkCampus offers services in multiple languages. Click here for more information about both of these services.
