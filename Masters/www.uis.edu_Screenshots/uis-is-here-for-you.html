Campus Support Services

Near or far, UIS is here for you!

Staying connected and available to our students is a top priority. Many support services remain available, though formats and hours of availability may have changed.

Bookstore

Bookstore

Email: 0193mgr@follett.com

Phone: 217-206-6766

RENTAL BOOKS

UIS Bookstore is not accepting walk ins.

Please see shopuis.com for TERM END RENTAL and BUYBACK INFO

See the Blue banner at top of page click on "MORE +" to see "HOW TO" create/print label and send rental returns by mail.

You have an additional 14 days to return rentals.

If you do not have a printer, pull up your label, call FedEx for a pick up, tell them that you need a "CALL TAG"  give them your tracking number

and they will bring your label when they come for pick up.

To order Fall 2020 and see all Bookstore Updates please see shopuis.com

Brookens Library

Brookens Library

Email: libcirc@uis.edu

Phone: 217-206-6605

The Library, including Archives and CINRC, will remain closed to physical traffic until further notice but online resources will continue to be available along with library services via email and chat. Follow on Twitter @UISLibrary  and Facebook at UIS Brookens Library.

Career Development Center

Career Development Center

Email: careerservices@uis.edu

Phone: 217-206-6508

Companies are still recruiting UIS students! See openings through your Career Connect account and check out the Jobs Links and Resources Page for additional opportunities.

 

Career Counseling/Career Advising appointments may be requested through students’ Career Connect account or by calling the office. Resume and cover letter reviews may be requested via email.

Wide varieties of resources are available on the website including the Career Toolbox and more.

Connect with the Career Center on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram for information, resources and tips daily. The center’s  YouTube channel also has video guides and tutorials.
