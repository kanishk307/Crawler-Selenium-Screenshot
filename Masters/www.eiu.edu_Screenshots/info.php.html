If You Are Experiencing Symptoms

If you are a student and you experience symptoms such as fever, cough, and shortness of breath, or if you feel you may be at risk for coronavirus, please contact the EIU Health and Counseling Services' Medical Clinic at (217) 581-3013. Others should contact their health care provider directly.
