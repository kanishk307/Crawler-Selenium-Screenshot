This is not meant to be an exhaustive list of all campus resources and services. All offices are working on getting their current information updated on their websites, so please visit their specific websites as needed.

Website for $_EscapeTool.xml($department.textValue)

Hours: M-F, 9am - 5pm

Contact Us:

How we can still help you:

At this time (as of 5 pm, Friday March 27 and ongoing), the Sherratt Library building is closed. We are still not open for academic exams. All faculty are to employ software-based proctoring solutions. If a student is in need of special accommodations, the student needs to work with their professor.

Students who need to take a placement exam, such as ALEKS or ACCUPLACER, they may email testing@suu.edu  to schedule an appointment for online or face to face proctoring. 

Specialized Testing is open to proctor FAA, CLEP, FLATS, and Praxis exams. Their hours are Monday-Thursday from 10:00am to 4:00pm. Please refer to Specialized Testing's website for information on how to register for these exams. Keep in mind that this may be subject to change.

Students entering the Testing Center and/or Specialized Testing, can be assured that SUU will follow CDC guidelines by implementing social distancing between students and limiting the number of students in the center. The Testing Center and Specialized Testing highly advises that all students follow proper social distancing and wear a face mask when entering and exiting the centers.

Undergraduate Writing Center

Email writingcenter@suu.edu

How we can still help you:

Summer Student Services:

  • Welcome! We are available to help students through the writing process and to assist them in making revisions and improvements to their writing.
  • Please make an appointment using the link. Sessions can include Zoom, Google docs, or whatever platform works best for the student.
    • Students should email a draft of the paper at least two hours before the session, along with a brief description of what they would like to work on.
    • Interactive sessions are preferred. If a student cannot be present during their scheduled appointment, a tutor will respond to an emailed draft during the session time and the draft will be returned to the student within one business day.
  • We will not be holding the Scriblerian writing contest this semester. We plan on holding the competition again in the Fall 2020 semester.
