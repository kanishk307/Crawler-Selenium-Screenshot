Student Wellness Services update

03/19/2020   Students  

Hi all!

Often emails begin with a statement such as "I hope this email finds you well."  

I sincerely I hope this is true for each one of you in this moment.

The staff here at Student Wellness services are experiencing many of the same things as I'm sure you are.  We worry about ourselves contracting this disease.  We are really worried about the more vulnerable members of our families and those that we love.  We feel the stress of having to change routines, deal with difficult financial realities, and face the uncertainty of what our lives, our society, and our world will look like over the next few months.  We wonder how well we will be able to cope.

At the same time we are extremely grateful to be a part of a community of people who care for and look out for each other.  And I am thankful to be part of a team of professionals that are committed to creativity, flexibility, and to providing high quality medical and mental health care.  To that end, our department is committed to protecting both the university community and our office staff from exposure.  Thus, we have transitioned from providing in-clinic services to the telehealth services listed below: 

  • If you have questions about or feel like you have been exposed to COVID-19 we will help direct you to local resources and information. However, if you are in distress, call 911 immediately.  Please note that our office is not equipped to provide testing. 
  • We will provide telemedicine with our medical team.
  • On the mental health side, students will now visit with their therapists through secure video conferencing.  
  • We are also working to build our online resource library and opportunities to connect and find support.

We will continue to be available by phone 951-785-2200 during normal business hours: M-Th 8 am - 5 pm, Fri 8 am - 12 pm.  You may also contact us by email: wellness@lasierra.edu.  And if you have any creative ideas on how we can do this more effectively, please let us know!

We are in this together and we will get through this together. 

Keep taking good care of yourselves and those around you.

Dan Nyirady, LMFT
