Athletic Department Resources

04/10/2020   Students   Faculty   Staff  

Dear Golden Eagles Family, welcome to Virtual Spring Quarter! We hope this email finds you safe and healthy at home.  

The Golden Eagles Administration Team, Coaches and La Sierra University Staff are continuing to transition operations online, ensuring continued communication and resources for our student-athletes during this unprecedented time. Even though we are not on campus together, we are still here for you. Please don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions or concerns. 

Student-Athletes, please see below for available resources:

La Sierra Together
La Sierra is more than a campus—it’s our community, family, and friends. Join us in the #LaSierraTogether movement as we participate in virtual events, talk about our experiences, and more. Be sure to tag your posts with #LaSierraTogether to share your adventures, challenges, and accomplishments with us! https://lasierra.edu/together/​​​​​​​
 
Message from the Director of Athletics, Javier Krumm
( jkrumm@lasierra.edu )
At La Sierra University, we are “servant leaders.” It is through servant leadership and hard work that we believe we can find success. The only place you will find the word success before work, is in the dictionary, otherwise success comes with hard work. It is important for us, during these times of crisis, our athletics program continues to develop our athletes physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually. “Today I will do what others won’t so tomorrow I can accomplish what others can’t.” - Jerry Rice

Message from the Faculty Athletics Representative, Dr. Arturo Diaz 
 ( adiaz@lasierra.edu )
Although athletics and school are important I want you to remember the big picture. This is a time to focus on, and be grateful for, your family and friend’s safety, health, and well-being. Even though this is not how we envisioned ending the school year, be proud of yourself, your resilience, and your ability to adapt to extreme circumstances. We are proud of each and every one of you! As you focus on your academics this quarter, please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any difficulties in any of your courses. 

Compliance: Brian Murphy, Head Athletic Trainer/Compliance 
 ( bmurphy@lasierra.edu )
 I want you to know we’re here for you in regards to eligibility, during this unique time. If you have any questions about staying eligible or other general eligibility inquiries, please don’t hesitate to contact me. IMPORTANT: Prior to dropping a class, please use the link below and fill out the “course withdrawal request.” By filling this out, we’ll be notified and able to look into your case to see if you’re able to drop the class. Not doing this before dropping a class may affect eligibility. Best of luck to you this quarter! Course withdrawal request

Academics: Office of Advising and Career Success (OACS)
( oacs@lasierra.edu )
 OACS offers numerous resources through Academic Advising, Recovery and Support, Career Services, the Collaborative Learning Center (CLC) and Disability Services, to name a few. Don’t hesitate to reach out to them for academic help.
