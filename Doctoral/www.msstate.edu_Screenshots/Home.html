Latest MSU COVID-19 Announcement (July 2, 2020)
MSU publishes ‘Safe Return’ guide for the fall semester

Mississippi State University is closely monitoring news and information related to COVID-2019, the worldwide health concern known as coronavirus. MSU officials are in regular contact with the Mississippi Department of Health and other health officials to take all precautions to protect students, faculty and staff.
