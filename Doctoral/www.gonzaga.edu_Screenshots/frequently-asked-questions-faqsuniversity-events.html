Will Commencement be held on September 4-6?

We clearly heard the call from our students and parents that an in-person celebration is important. Plans are underway for ceremonies that meet federal and state safety concerns, which may result in a new schedule and limited guests. Updates will be shared with graduated/graduating students as soon as possible.
 

Where can I get updates about Commencement?

Comprehensive information on 2020 Commencement exercises, scheduled for Sept. 4-6, 2020, can be found at our dedicated website: www.gonzaga.edu/commencement. Information on that site will be updated as needed throughout the summer.

Please contact Angela Ruff, Manager of Academic Events, with any questions:
Phone: (509) 313-3572;
Email: ruff@gonzaga.edu.

 

How do I get my cap and gown?

Bachelors, Master’s and Law students can purchase the ceremony rental attire from Herff Jones.
