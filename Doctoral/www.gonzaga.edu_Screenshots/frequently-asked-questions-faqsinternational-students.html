Will Gonzaga allow for a late arrival for the Fall 2020 semester?

Yes, the university will work with students who need a late arrival due to visa or international travel restrictions.

Please contact isss@gonzaga.edu for more information.

 

If I am unable to arrive on campus in time for classes to begin and I do not want to defer my start, do I have the option to begin my first semester of courses for Fall 2020 online?

Courses for English Language Center students will be scheduled for synchronous meetings that will meet either in person or online, depending on the ability and needs of students and faculty. The decision to meet in person or online will be made collaboratively and may be adjusted as needed as the session unfolds.

In alignment with University policy, all courses will be recorded via Zoom, unless we find ourselves in the unlikely situation that everyone is able to safely be physically present.

Students who enroll and cannot travel to the Spokane campus can be full participants in the course. However, they may be required to attend online, in synchronous time (Spokane, Pacific Daylight), in order to fully participate. Decisions regarding any deviation from synchronous attendance will be made by the course instructor, in consultation with students and the academic coordinator.

 

What about my Immigration Status?

Students who will travel to campus for the Fall 2020 semester will be issued an I-20 for Fall 2020.

Students who are not able to travel to campus for the Fall 2020 semester and, instead, will be studying remotely from home, will be issued an I-20 for Spring 2021. This will allow travel to the US to attend courses in person.

If you have any questions about your I-20 or immigration status, please contact International Student & Scholar Services at isss@gonzaga.edu.

 

When I arrive on campus for Fall 2020, do I need to go into quarantine?

Yes. Any students who cross an international border (by any means, including plane, train, car, bus, etc.) in order to arrive on campus for classes (for Summer 2020 and Fall 2020) are required to participate in a two-week quarantine.

Students who plan to live on campus can arrive as early as August 13 in order to complete quarantine prior to the beginning of the fall semester. Students who have off-campus housing for Summer 2020 or Fall 2020 must complete their quarantine off campus.
 

When I arrive in Spokane, I will need purchase bedding and personal supplies for my residence hall. How will I do these things while under quarantine?

Students will be able to order through our college dorm outfitting program provided by the Parent & Family Relations Office. Simply select the items needed and they will be delivered to your residence hall.

Place an order  

 

 

I currently have belongings stored by Housing & Residence Life and when I arrive on campus, I must go directly into a 2-week quarantine. How do I get my stuff?

You will be able to contact Plant & Construction Services to make an appointment to retrieve your belongings after your two-week quarantine.

To make your appointment please call Plant & Construction Services at (509) 313-5656. They will inform you of how many items/boxes you have, and you will need to plan your transportation accordingly.

 

What will Fall 2020 International Student Orientation look like?

Due to COVID-19 related concerns, and the mandatory two-week quarantine Fall 2020 orientation will be hosted online. Detailed information will be coming at the end of July.
 

Does my International Student Insurance cover COVID-19 related care? And, how do I use my insurance coverage?

Yes. Your Lewermark health insurance covers COVID-19 related care and testing. Information about how to use your student insurance will be part of your online orientation.

If you have any questions, please contact Britt Harmon at harmonb2@gonzaga.edu.
