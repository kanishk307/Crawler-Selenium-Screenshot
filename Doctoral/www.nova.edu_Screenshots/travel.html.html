Travel

  • Students returning from NSU-related travel, vacation or spring break from any of the CDC Level-3 countries that have been identified (China, Italy, Iran, South Korea) were self-quarantined for 14 days.
  • Anthony Fauci, head of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Disease has encouraged the elderly and those with underlying medical issues to suspend their travel plans.
  • All non-essential NSU air travel has been suspended. We urge extreme caution and judgment for your personal domestic and international travel plans.
  • Essential travel will be determined by your respective dean (for students) or vice president (for employees).
