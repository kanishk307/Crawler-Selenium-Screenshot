UTSA Virtual Backgrounds for Zoom

Zoom is a popular platform for video conferencing, and we know many UTSA students, faculty and staff use it for personal and professional meetings. 

Zoom's virtual background feature allows you to display a custom image as your background during meetings. We created a handful of UTSA-inspired virtual backgrounds so you can show off your Roadrunner pride while Zooming. Enjoy!

How to Download

  1. Click on the image of your choice
  2. Right click on the full version of the image
  3. Click "Save image as" and save it to your desired location (desktop, downloads, a folder, etc.)

How to Set Downloaded Image as Backdrop in Zoom

Once you've launched a Zoom meeting:

  1. Click on ^ next to "Stop Video" on the bottom left of your Zoom window and select "Choose Virtual Background"
  2. Click on the + on the right-hand side below the current image in your settings
  3. Click "Add Image"
  4. Find your saved image and select it
  5. You may have to deselect "mirror my video” in the video settings tab. This setting will cause the logo/image to appear backward to you, however, the users you are talking to will see it displayed correctly.
