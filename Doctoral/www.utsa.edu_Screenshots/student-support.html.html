 24/7 BLACKBOARD SUPPORT

First line 24/7 support for Blackboard is now available to all faculty, staff and students.

  • Phone: Call 210-458-4520 and press option 1 to access Blackboard Support anytime.
  • Email: Blackboard Support can be emailed directly at utsa@edusupportcenter.com (for faster service, please use your UTSA email account)
  • Chat: coming soon
  • Self-help portal: coming soon

These channels are dedicated to our UTSA Blackboard environment specifically. Escalated tickets will be handled Monday-Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., excluding holidays. Comments or concerns? Contact academicinnovation@utsa.edu .

Counseling & Mental Health

Schedule online counseling sessions Monday-Friday 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. with Counseling and Mental Health Services. Call 210-458-4140 (option 2) or use their TAO mobile app.

Stay Well with Campus Rec

Stay active with fun, engaging activities offered through Campus Rec – from live group exercise classes, to one-on-one nutrition consultations, to fitness and wellness activities you can do from home.

Student Health

Student Health Services is providing telehealth services for all currently enrolled students. Call 210-458-4142 weekdays from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. for a consultation.

Roadrunner Pantry

The Main Campus pantry is now operating on a self-service model. Guests will be asked to log in their personal information on site. Anyone with a UTSA ID can stop by weekdays from noon to 4 p.m. to get up to seven bags of food, limited to one visit per week. If you have questions, email RoadrunnerPantry@utsa.edu.

Financial Assistance

If you are facing financial hardship due to the impact of the coronavirus pandemic, be sure to visit the Financial Assistance Resources for UTSA Students. It includes instructions on how to apply for emergency funds as well as information on several other university and community resources available to help you.
