What's Open on Campus

Current for Summer 2020

UTSA has modified its operations for the remainder of the spring semester due to COVID-19. The university has moved all classes online, and the majority of faculty and staff are working remotely.

To the extent possible, campus services have been virtualized. Below is a list of campus offices and services and their operating status.
