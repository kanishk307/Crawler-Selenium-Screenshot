Roadrunner Relief

Students and families are struggling financially. UTSA has resources to help.

Now more than ever, we are committed to keeping our Roadrunners on the path to achieving their educational goals. If you’re facing financial hardship due to the pandemic, we have funds and other resources to support you.

Need help navigating the emergency aid process? Chat with a One Stop counselor using the widget below.

CARES Act Funding & Emergency Aid

UTSA received $14 million from the federal CARES Act to distribute to students needing emergency aid as a result of the pandemic. Fund distribution is underway for students who have already submitted an emergency aid application, and there still is funding available.

Food Pantry

The Roadrunner Pantry provides healthy food, free of charge, to students in need. The pantry’s Main Campus location in the Student Union is currently providing grab-and-go bags of food – check the website for latest operating hours.
