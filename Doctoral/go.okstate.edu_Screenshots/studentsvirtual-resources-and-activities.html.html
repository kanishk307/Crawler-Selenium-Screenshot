Virtual Resources and Activities

Don’t let being away from campus keep you from being involved! Check out this list of resources and virtual activities you can partake in from your home!

Resources for students

Library Resources

Access academic resources, like the library’s 54 databases, 130,000 e-serials, 3.2 million e-books and other electronic resources, including the library’s 65 digital collections, online.

Food

If you are needing help with groceries, Our Daily Bread is giving our pre-packaged boxes of food on a drive through basis. For more information, check out their website.

Career Services

Career Services can assist you with various career-related questions and assistance virtually! Join Career Services for a four-week virtual series, Couch to Careers, on how to gain skills while being an “online-only” student.

Local Activities

Explore everything virtually below!

Virtual Engagement

The Student Union Activities Board

The Student Union Activities Board still has weekly events, just online now! Check out our social media to stay updated on everything we are doing until the end of the semester! @suabokstate on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

1 is 2 Many

During this uncharted and confusing time 1 is 2 Many is still committed to supporting students.  1 is 2 Many has also transitioned to virtual learning experiences.  Be sure to follow us @okstate1is2many to stay up to date with our events.

Prairie Arts Center

Prairie Arts Center in Stillwater has daily art prompts and how to videos of arts projects made with household items.

Sur la Table

Sur la Table is a cooking school in Oklahoma City, they have uploaded tutorial videos for people to watch at home and create a variety of different dishes

Chat on Discord

OSU students are connecting with each other through Discord! This chatting platform helps students interact through messages and picture sharing. Create an account and join the conversation.

Online Museum Exhibits

National Cowboy & Western Heritage Museum

The National Cowboy & Western Heritage Museum in Oklahoma City has created multiple online exhibits consisting of videos and pictures of artifacts stored in their museum.

Oklahoma Hall of Fame

The Oklahoma Hall of Fame in Oklahoma City has created a variety of different virtual activities for anyone to engage in online.

Oklahoma National Memorial Museum

The Oklahoma National Memorial Museum has created a virtual hope trunk so people can still see the artifacts stored in the museum.

Animals

The Tulsa Zoo

The Tulsa zoo is doing a series of facebook live events for people to engage with them from home. They have different live events everyday and provide the links on their website. They also have different learning activities anyone can partake in.

Oklahoma City Zoo

The Oklahoma City Zoo has created a variety of online activities for the public to engage in from their homes. One thing they have started is OKC Zoo @ Two. Each day they post a video with various topics at 2 p.m.

Wellness

TAO University Counseling Services

TAO is an online course that helps people deal with issues. They have modules to help with anxiety, depression, relationships, and improving your mood.

Campus Counseling Services

University counseling is still offering their services online. Their website has their updated services available!

OSU Group Fitness Classes

The wellness center offers exercise classes that you can do at home.

Healthy Recipes

Shape your future Oklahoma has created a list of healthy recipes to try.

Exercise Library

The Department of Wellness has a virtual library of tons of easy exercises you can do at home.
