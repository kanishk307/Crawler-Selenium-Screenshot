Registrar Update

As spring classes resume online and summer/fall 2020 enrollment begins, the Registrar’s Office is eager to support you. We’ve compiled updates to procedures and spring deadlines to help you with your next steps.

 

  • Please submit forms and communicate with us electronically. Our team is available to assist you via email at registrar@okstate.edu. You can also call us at 405.744.6876 and leave a message, and your call will be returned as quickly as possible.
  • Approvals from OSU faculty and staff received via an OSU email account will be accepted in place of signatures on forms.
  • With a goal of supporting students in successful completion of their spring coursework, we are extending the following spring 2020 deadlines to allow students more flexibility accommodating to recent changes:
  • Spring graduation application deadline – now Friday, April 24
  • W drop/withdraw deadline (drop one or more regular 16-week spring classes with an automatic grade of W) – now Friday, April 24*
  • Please consult with your advisor before dropping a spring class.
  • Priority enrollment for summer and fall 2020 has begun! Find your enrollment date in the Notes section of your Student Profile in Self-Service, or on our website.
  • Remember that you can request official transcripts and enrollment certifications online from Self-Service.
  • Spring 2020 degree candidates will be given a few weeks’ leniency with the graduation deadline to submit all degree requirement documentation (e.g. grade changes, transcripts from other institutions, etc.)

*To find unique extended deadlines for spring short courses, please visit our short course deadlines website
