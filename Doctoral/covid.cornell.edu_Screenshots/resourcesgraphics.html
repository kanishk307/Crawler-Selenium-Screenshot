Signage & Graphics

To help keep the Cornell community informed about COVID-19, all colleges, schools and units are encouraged to use and distribute the items below rather than creating their own. The more consistent our messaging, the better we can reach our many audiences and help everyone to stay informed, safe and healthy.

You may download and print the posters for your area and use the digital signage for your online presence. If you would like to have the vinyl floor decals or posters professionally printed, please contact Cornell Print Services at printservices@cornell.edu to receive a detailed list and order form.
