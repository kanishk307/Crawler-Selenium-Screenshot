Daily Check

Cornell University requires those approved to work or conduct research on the Ithaca campus or related facilities to complete the Daily Check prior to their arrival each day.

Check in

The State of New York has issued guidance that mandates daily health screenings. In an effort to comply with this mandate, the university has developed Daily Check, an easy-to-use online tool that allows faculty, staff and students approved to work or conduct research on Cornell’s main campus or related facilities to confirm that they are symptom-free and have not had recent exposure to someone with COVID-19 prior to their arrival on campus or accessing university facilities.

All faculty, staff and students who have been approved to resume on-campus work or research through the university’s reactivation process are required to complete the Daily Check each day before arriving on campus or accessing campus facilities. If you have questions about whether you have been cleared to return to campus, please contact your department chair, supervisor or human resources representative.

If you have a disability and are having trouble accessing information on this website or need materials in an alternate format, contact web-accessibility@cornell.edu
