Latest Communication from Provost Sue Ott Rowlands

March 31, 2020 - Read More

Dear Colleagues, 

Thank you for your continuing flexibility and diligent work to make our transition to alternative instruction a success. COVID-19 has presented an unprecedented challenge for our university and you have met it with courage, sharing resources and helping each other.

We have put together a centralized site for instructional resources and information during this time of transition. Please click Alternative Teaching Resources to the right for resources and let our office know if you have additional questions.

You can email Jason Vest at vestj3@nku.edu or call 859-572-5622.

Best,

Sue Ott Rowlands
Provost and Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs
