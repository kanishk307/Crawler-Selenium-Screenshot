Coronavirus Information (COVID-19)

Due to national guidance on the coronavirus (COVID-19), and remain in compliance with university and state policies, all visits are canceled through May 1. We too are disappointed to cancel these campus experiences, so we've created options for you to virtually visit campus.

Our team is available to answer any questions you may have. Contact us at beanorse@nku.edu and 859-572-5220 or 1-(800) 637-9948.

This information may change in the coming days and weeks. Please check back often or reach out to your admissions counselor.

Effective April 17 - August 17, 2020, all applications fees for Fall 2020 will be waived. The fee waiver will occur automatically when you submit the application.

Answers to University Housing Questions 

How can I get my questions answered?
Contact the Office of Admissions at beanorse@nku.edu or 859-572-5220 and we will assist you.
Are you processing applications for admission?
Yes. As of now, all admissions functions except for on-campus programs are continuing as normal.
Will this affect timing of admission notification?
No. As of now, this does not change our plans for any decisions coming from our office.
My current school is closed. Will you accept an unofficial transcript?
We can accept unofficial transcripts to make preliminary decisions. Continued enrollment at NKU is contingent upon receipt of a final official transcript.
Will more visit dates be added later?
We are currently offering virtual visits. We will continue to monitor our spring visit schedule and CDC recommendations to accommodate as many visitors as possible. 
If I can't come to campus to visit, what are my other options?
While we aren’t currently accepting individual or group visits on campus, you can watch our virtual tour or register for a virtual visit. Contact your counselor with any questions you have about becoming a Norse! 
NKU Admissions is scheduled to visit my area. Will they still be there?
As of now, all NKU Admissions related travel is suspended.
Will I be allowed to step foot on campus?
Our office is not offering phyisical tours or information sessions at this time. We encourage you to reschedule your visit when our programs resume or "attend" a virtual visit. 
I am registered for a daily visit (Norse Day). Should I go?
We hope you can physically join us at a later date, in the meantime our Norse Days have transitioned to a virtual experience and all registrants will be invited to join us online.
Will Northern Exposure: Registration dates change?
At this time we do not expect Registration dates to be affected.
Will the freshmen scholarship deadline be extended?
Yes. The admission deadline for Kentucky and Indiana Rate students to be considered for merit scholarships has changed to June 1, 2020.  No additional application is required. Out-of-state students are considered for the EDGE Award on a rolling basis, thus there is no deadline to be considered for the award.
Will the May 1 deadline to confirm enrollment to NKU be extended?

Yes. Our confirmation deadline has changed to June 1, 2020.

I have been selected as a Summer 2020 GPS/GSA/GSE participant. Will scholarships be impacted if the program is canceled?
Any high school student selected for the Summer 2020 Governor’s Scholars Program, Governor’s School for the Arts,  and/or Governor’s School for Entrepreneurs who meets the scholarship criteria at NKU, will qualify for the GSP, GSA and GSE award for Fall 2021 or future terms, even if the summer programs are not able to be held due to COVID-19.
Are other offices at NKU open?

Yes. You are encouraged to contact respective offices via email or phone call with any questions or concerns.

Office of Student Financial Assistance: ofa@nku.edu, 859-572-5143 or toll free at 1-888-225-4499.
NKU Housing: housing@nku.edu, 859-572-5676 or toll free at 1-866-572-5676.
