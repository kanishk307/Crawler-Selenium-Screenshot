    New Student Orientation

    New Student Orientation and Parent Orientation will take place virtually in a live format on the student and parent’s scheduled orientation day. In addition, the Office of Orientation and Transition Programs will offer pre-orientation modules, as well as asynchronous sessions throughout the summer with campus partners. We are confident that our offerings – both live and asynchronous – will help us to seamlessly welcome our new Fall 2020 students!

    Full details on New Student Orientation can be found at orientation.kennesaw.edu. If you plan to begin at KSU this Fall but you’ve not yet registered for Orientation, register here: https://orientation.kennesaw.edu/registration.php.

    We look forward to virtually welcoming you this summer, and seeing you face-to-face this Fall! If you have any questions, please email Orientation and Transition Programs at KSUOrientation@kennesaw.edu.
