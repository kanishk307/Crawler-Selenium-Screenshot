Prospective Undergraduate Students

I’m facing challenges in completing my application to Drexel University, due to COVID-19. What support are you able to offer?

If you will be unable to complete your application by the program’s deadline, please contact us at enroll@drexel.edu so that we can discuss possible solutions to any complications you may be experiencing.

I was hoping to come visit campus in person – are you still offering on-campus events and tours?

Many in-person admissions events have been suspended, but we are excited to offer you a wide variety of virtual opportunities.

What is the best way to contact the Office of Admissions?

Due to our limited in-office staffing, you can also speak with an admissions representative through our all-day chats, reach out to us via email at enroll@drexel.edu, or connect with your undergraduate admissions representative.

Prospective Graduate Students

I’m facing challenges in completing my application to Drexel University, due to COVID-19. What support are you able to offer?

If you will be unable to complete your application by the program’s deadline, please contact us at enroll@drexel.edu so that we can discuss possible solutions to any complications you may be experiencing.

I was hoping to come visit campus in person – are you still offering on-campus events and tours?

All in-person admissions events have been postponed, but we are excited to offer you a wide variety of virtual opportunities both from graduate admissions and our individual colleges.

You may also want to contact your school or department directly to speak with someone specific to your degree.

What is the best way to contact the Office of Admissions?

Due to our limited in-office staffing, you can reach out to us via email at enroll@drexel.edu, or connect with your graduate admissions representative.
