Media Resources

 

UAB provides media briefings as needed. Above is a playlist of the briefings on COVID-19 since January 30, 2020. Click on the top right corner to view the full playlist.

UAB has multiple experts available to discuss a wide variety of topics related to COVID-19, including symptoms, spread of germs, vaccine development, preparedness and more.

See our experts here.

UAB’s public relations specialists are the fastest, most efficient way for reporters to reach UAB experts on deadline. Call 205-934-3884 or page the media specialist on call at 205-934-3411.

The UAB News Studio is available for live or recorded HD video, audio and Skype interviews with UAB experts worldwide through LTN Global Networks. The studio is equipped with an ISDN line for digital radio interviews.
