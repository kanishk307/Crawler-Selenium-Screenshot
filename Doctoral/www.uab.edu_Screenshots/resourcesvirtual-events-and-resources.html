Virtual Events and Resources

Looking for things to do as we social distance? There's still a lot going on at UAB—from workout classes to movie nights to virtual art exhibits, even live coloring sessions. Plus, all these virtual events and resources are open to everyone—so share with them with your friends and neighbors.

    Virtual Resources

    Arts & Entertainment

    • Art Play Classes

      The Alys Stephens Center is offering several virtual Art Play classes and camps this summer. From visual arts to creative writing, your next [virtual] arts experience is just a click away! Check out the class lineup HERE and register now! **LIMITED VIRTUAL SPOTS AVAILABLE / CLASS**

    • AIM at Home

      Since we are all family, please enjoy an Arts in Medicine experience from wherever you are. Click the link to join Kim McKenzie, one of the AIM artists in residence, as she guides you on how to be present in the moment.

    • Blaze Coloring Sheet

      Take a break with your family from work or school and color in Blaze! Take a pic of your creation and then post it on social media and tag @uabalumni.

    • Alumni Work From Home Playlist

      Enjoy this National Alumni Society work from home playlist on Spotify.

    • ODEI Social DisDancing Playlist

      Playlist of songs the Office of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion have been listening to while working from home.

    • Social Distancing Sculptures

      Assistant Professor of Sculpture Stacey Holloway is creating a series of sculptures related to social distancing.

    Educational

    • Help UAB Respond to COVID-19

      Whether it's fueling research, supporting patient care, helping a student in need, or uplifting a UAB employee, we need your help in the fight against COVID-19. Here are ways you can support the university's response to the coronavirus pandemic.

    • Show Caregivers Appreciation

      Express your support for all the employees on the front line of the defense against COVID-19.

    • UAB Counts

      UAB could receive less money to help support education, research, health care, and more if we underrepresent ourselves in the Census. Here are a few examples of programs and initiatives that could be diminished if you don’t stand up to be counted. Make your voice heard in the 2020 Census.

    • Zoom Backgrounds

      Discover virtual backgrounds with univeristy branding for your Zoom calls.

    • Blazers Working Well

      Highlighting employees who are continuing their mission-critical work — remotely or among the few employees still present on campus.

    • TechKnow Podcast

      Recent special episode about tech for working remotely. There is also a hub of remote working tech at go.uab.edu/remotework that will have some tech videos and infographics soon.

    • Community Heroes: Recognize A Hero

      We want to recognize those who are on the front lines fighting the novel coronavirus (COVID-19), and your stories and words of encouragement will go a long way in showing support and gratitude.

    • Electronic Portal for Assistance Learning

      EPAL is to provide educators access to high-quality online resources to remediate student difficulties in math and literacy. EPAL is unique in that the resources have been categorized by student error. This categorization facilitates educators selecting resources that better meet student needs.

    • Small Biz Blazers

      We would like to help our alumni owned or run small businesses spread the word on how our community can help you during this unprecedented time. Visit the Alumni small business directory, and fill-out a short informational form on how our community can support you.

    • Front Line Blazers

      Do you know a Blazer doing extraordinary things on the front lines of COVID-19? The National Alumni Society are featuring some of our alums giving back to the community during this unprecedented time in history. Post it on Facebook and hashtag #FrontLineBlazers.

    • Green and Told Playlist

      UAB Green and Told is a biweekly podcast that shares the stories from members of the UAB community. Through Green and Told, the UAB Office of Alumni Affairs sit down with alumni, faculty, students and others.

    • School of Nursing response across missions

      Learn how the School of Nursing has adjusted to new normal across its three missions.

    • ODEI Diversity Education Webinar

      ODEI is offering Diversity Education available via webinar. These courses will be offered as a webinar via Zoom. Available dates and registration for these courses will be accessible through Campus LMS. Registration for these courses is required through Campus LMS.

    • Nursing Heroes

      The UAB Nursing Partnership is collecting the stories of how our UAB Nurses -- UABSON Alumni and UAB Medicine Nurses are making a difference in the fight against COVID-19 in Alabama and around the world.

    • MedCast Podcast

      System for those in medical profession (specifically non-essential) to earn CME credits at home via podcast.

    • Invent it. Disclose it.

      Harbert Institute Campaign designed to drive Intellectual Property (IP) disclosures. See how UAB inventors are turning university IP into life-changing products and services.

    Fitness

    • URec UFit Virtual Fitness Classes

      Join your favorite UFit instructors for FREE Virtual UFit classes from the comfort of your home. As Blazers, you have access to over 90 Virtual Group Exercise classes each week due to our partnership with friends and partners at institutions across the country.

    • Les Mills Fitness Classes

      Les Mills – At home workouts brought to you by University Recreation! You have access to over 100 FREE workouts during the COVID-19 situation. There are 10 workout categories varying from High-Intensity Interval Training to classes focused on mindfulness.

    Wellness

    • Mental Health Tools

      During National Mental Health Month, the Employee Assistance and Counseling Center is providing the UAB community with tools on a variety of topics to aid mental health. Click here to see all they are offering and make sure to check back each week.

    • Wellness Brigade

      Curating wellness content in one resource across 5 domains UAB Medicine Wellness supports (David Rogers - Chief Wellness Officer).

    • Guided Meditations

      Zoom meditations for students to unwind and relax between classes. Monday Mindfulness @ 11:00 - 11:30 am and Friday Focus @ 2:00 - 2:30 pm. Zoom Meeting ID: 440 253 2164.
