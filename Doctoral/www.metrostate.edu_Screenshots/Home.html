Grading change for Summer 2020 courses

Students: If you wish to request a change to the grading for your Summer 2020 courses from letter (A through F) grades to Competence (S)/No Competence (NC), complete the Summer 2020 Grade Change Form.

Building Hours, Access and Parking

  • Student Service Offices, Computer Lab Temporary Hours, and Building Access

    A list of students services with available hours and contact information, as well as computer lab locations and hours.

  • Temporary Building Hours, Access, and Parking Information

    Information about building access for the Saint Paul Campus, Management Education Center (MEC), Midway Center, and the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Education Center in Brooklyn Park, as well as updated parking information for the Saint Paul Campus.

    Students should refer Student Service Offices, Computer Lab Temporary Hours, and Building Access for access and computer lab availability.

Report an Instance of COVID-19

Students and employees are requested to report exposure to, or a diagnosis of, COVID-19 to the university.

Resources for Physical and Mental Health

  • How to Protect Yourself

    Information about protecting yourself from COVID-19 in English, Spanish and Chinese. From the Centers for Disease Control.

  • For students: 24/7 Counseling Services Help Line

    Metro State students can call 651-793-1568 any time, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to speak to a licensed counselor.

  • For employees: EAP Work/Life Counseling

    A free service for employees of the State of Minnesota. Call 651-259-3840 or 800-657-3719 any time. Online access also available.

  • CDC: Manage Anxiety and Stress

    Tips on coping with stress during this pandemic.

  • Tips for Social Distancing, Quarantine, and Isolation

    PDF. Tips on taking care of your behavioral health from the U.S. Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration.
