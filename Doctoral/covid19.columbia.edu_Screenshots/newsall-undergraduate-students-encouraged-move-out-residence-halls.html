All Undergraduate Students Encouraged to Move Out of Residence Halls

Today, the University announced all classes for the remainder of the semester will be conducted online. Students who are able to move out of undergraduate residence halls for the rest of the semester are encouraged to do so.

Editor's note:

FAQs were updated on 3/12/2020 to include guidance on Room Selection for the Fall Term.

By
Columbia Housing
March 12, 2020

All students must complete the March 2020 Housing Status form on the Housing Portal, as soon as possible but no later than Monday, March 16 at 5:00 p.m. EDT.

This will help us maintain accurate occupancy records so that we can provide support services. Those who are able to are encouraged to complete an early check-out from the residence halls by Monday, March 30.

Click here for a list of frequently asked questions regarding today's guidance and the process for Columbia College and Columbia Engineering undergraduates to move out of their residence halls.
