Child Care & Schooling

The Office of Work/Life is Here for You

The School & Child Care Search Service recognizes how extraordinarily challenging this time is for many families. While we cannot change the realities, we are staying as informed as possible. Columbia colleagues, we are here to think out loud with you about the particulars of your situation and explore options.  

Schools & Child Care Closed by State Mandate

Pursuant to state-level mandates, all NYC-area schools and child care programs are currently prohibited from providing any in-person instruction or care.*  

Summer Camp

The availability of in-person and virtual summer programming is evolving rapidly.  Check with individual camps and stay tuned to state-level announcements.  See our Summer Resource Guide.

Stay Informed

Changes in policy may evolve at different rates in various communities around the tri-state area. We urge you to stay informed by listening to local and state-level news.

Check Back for Updates

Continue to check this website for updates and helpful information. Columbia colleagues and students, please complete our online information request form if you would like to connect with us.  

*The only on-site programming currently available for children in NYC is at centers restricted to essential workers and Department of Education Regional Enrichment Centers (RECs), which also accommodate only children of essential workers.

School & Child Care Search Service

Expertise and guidance as you explore early education and child care or K-12 schooling options

Back-Up Care Program

Care for adults, children, or yourself in situations when normal care arrangements are interrupted or when short-term care is required

Child Care Benefit

Eligible officers can receive a contribution from Columbia University to a Dependent Care Flexible Spending Account.  (Click on Dependent Care FSA and scroll to the bottom of the section.) Recipients can then get reimbursed for child care expenses through this account.
