Dining

Information on dining options available on campus and in the surrounding neighborhood. Neighborhood business hours are subject to change.

Undergraduates

Dining operations will pause for summer starting May 16.

CUIMC

The CUIMC student meal plan will continue to serve on the same schedule, with meals to go, as dining service is suspended.

Neighborhood Options

Many local grocers and restaurants are open for takeout and delivery orders; note that hours are subject to change. For Columbia students, the Food Pantry, inside JJ’s Place on the lower level of John Jay Hall, is open and accepting donations every Tuesday and Friday from 3:00 pm to 6:00 pm.

In addition a number of neighborhood eateries are open, and some accept Flex.
