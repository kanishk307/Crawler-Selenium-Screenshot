Facilities and Campus Life

Facilities and Campus Life

establishing health and safety protocols for campus operations while COVID-19 mitigation strategies are in place

The Facilities and Campus Life Working Group provides direct support for schools in their plans for reopening. This includes the development of interim space use and modification guidelines, templates for risk mitigation, assessment of mechanical systems and recommended operating protocols, and custodial operating protocols, as well as a program to promote adherence to public health guidelines.

These efforts proceed in collaboration with the working groups for Public Health, Research, and Academic Planning. Together, we consider all major issues related to the upcoming year and will establish frameworks for the University to resume operations.

How Campus Life Will Be Different

Facilities and Operations has implemented several changes to improve health and safety on campus. Check back frequently for updates.

Cleaning

Enhanced cleaning and disinfecting that meets or exceeds CDC, New York State Department of Health, and OSHA standards

Elevators

Elevator capacity will be limited. Follow the instructions on the sign nearby whatever elevator you plan to use.

Outdoor Space Use

On campus and in outdoor spaces, everyone must follow University health policy.

Workspace

Workspaces now have a combination of measures in place to reduce density. Follow workplace and shared-space hygiene practices.
