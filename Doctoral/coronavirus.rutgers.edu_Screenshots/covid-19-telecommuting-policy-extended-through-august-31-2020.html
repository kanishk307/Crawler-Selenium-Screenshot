COVID-19 Telecommuting – Extended through August 31, 2020

June 19, 2020

Dear Rutgers Colleagues,

In order to continue to support social distancing, the telecommuting rule relaxation policy set to expire on June 30, 2020 has been extended through August 31, 2020. Telecommuting is automatically updated and does not require a new or revised request unless there has been a change to your work schedule.

Sincerely,

Vivian Fernández
Senior Vice President for Human Resources & Organizational Effectiveness
