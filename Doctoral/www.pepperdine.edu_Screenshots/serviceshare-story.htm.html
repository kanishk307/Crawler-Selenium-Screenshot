Share a Story of Service During COVID-19

During good times and bad, the Pepperdine community demonstrates its commitment to serving others in ways that are uniquely Pepperdine. In an effort to shine a much-needed light in a time of darkness, we would like to share how you or other members of the Waves family are caring for your communities during the COVID-19 crisis. From launching a grassroots volunteering operation to fighting the disease on the frontlines, please share your story by filling out the form below with as much information as possible so we may uplift one another with hope and solidarity as we overcome this challenging time together.
