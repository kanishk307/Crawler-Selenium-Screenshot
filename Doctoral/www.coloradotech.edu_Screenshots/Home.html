Colorado Technical Universityâ€™s Response to COVID-19

Amid the COVID-19 health situation, Colorado Technical University remains open and dedicated to serving new and returning students during these rapidly changing times.

We do not anticipate any interruptions to your educational journey, and we will continue to be available to assist you while still providing the same level of outstanding service you expect when joining the CTU family.

The health and well-being of our students, faculty and staff are of the utmost importance to us. Therefore, the University has transitioned all campus-based classes in Colorado to an online or remote setting. All fully-online courses remain unaffected.

Despite the challenges COVID-19 presents, CTU is confident we can keep our students, faculty and staff safe while simultaneously supporting you in your educational goals.
