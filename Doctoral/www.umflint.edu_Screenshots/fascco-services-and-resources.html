      Additional resources are being provided as we continue to work to support our campus community and each other in these unprecedented times. As these resources become available, we will update the Flint HR website.

      FASCCO Counseling Sessions

      We would like to remind you during this challenging time, of the short-term, no charge, counseling services that are available via the Faculty and Staff Counseling and Consultation Office (FASCCO). FASCCO Affiliate counselors in the Flint area, as well as other FASCCO counselors, are set up for tele-health video sessions or phone counseling sessions. The staff of FASCCO can also assist you in identifying behavioral/mental health counselors that are part of your health plans and who are providing tele-health services. To obtain authorization for these services just contact FASCCO at fascco@umich.edu (preferred method) or call 734-936-8660 and one of their administrative assistants will assist you in obtaining services.

      In addition to the counseling services available, other supportive resources, including those for mental health, for faculty and staff can be found at: 

      • The Center for Positive Organizations
      • Guide to Well-Being During the Covid-19 Pandemic from UM Human Resources
      • UM-Flint Professional Development Opportunities
