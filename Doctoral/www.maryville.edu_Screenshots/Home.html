Saints Strong

100% Committed to Your Success!

Maryville Is SaintStrong
and Welcomes You This Fall.

Maryville University will reopen its campus for the Fall 2020 semester with a flexible, hybrid academic experience that educates, empowers and prepares students for future careers. The health, safety and well-being of students is our highest priority, and we are excited to move forward — together.

Announcements
Frequently Asked Questions

Creating a COVID-19 ready campus

  • Implementing social distancing requirements and infection-prevention measures.
  • Enhancing cleaning protocols throughout campus.
  • Expanding health and wellness services and adding resources for COVID-19 testing.

Delivering Flexible, Hybrid Academics

  • Providing a combination of in-person and virtual coursework.
  • Concluding on-campus instruction on November 20 so students may finish the semester at home through virtual learning.
  • Launching Online Plus classes, 16-week foundation courses of 100% online learning.

Providing a modified living experience

  • Limiting the number of students living on-campus and offering supplemental housing at local hotels.
  • Reserving specific buildings for students to safely quarantine, if needed.
  • Implementing an enhanced dining service with a mobile ordering system, eliminating self-serve stations, and expanding the pre-packaged meal options.
We are dedicated to providing a transformational educational experience that empowers our graduates to become lifelong learners and engaged, successful members of their communities. Together, we are SaintStrong.
— Dr. Mark Lombardi
President of Maryville University
