IT Service Desk

You can still get help on technology through chat, email or phone.

Website: https://it.usu.edu/service-desk

USU Registrar’s Office

Find information about pass/fail and class withdrawal deadlines and registration changes.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/registrar/covid/

SNAC Pantry

USU’s student food pantry, SNAC, has moved services to online ordering and curbside pickup available at the Logan campus for all students.

Website: https://servicecenter.usu.edu/programs/snac

Aggie Math Learning Center | Math and Statistics Tutoring Lab

All math and statistics tutoring sessions for spring semester 2020 have been moved to online delivery. Access your virtual math or stats class tutoring sessions.

Website: https://www.math.usu.edu/learningcenter/amlc

USU Writing Center

USU Writing Center services are available online, schedule a virtual appointment.

Website: https://writing.usu.edu/

Academic Advising

Schedule a virtual appointment with your academic advisor.

Website: https://advising.usu.edu/

Latinx Cultural Center

Latinx Cultural Center is operating remotely and remains a resource to students. Students may reach out to Pam Allcott at 435-797-0446 or pam.allcott@usu.edu.

Website: https://latinx.usu.edu/

Library Services

Library Services at USU campuses across the state are accessible through adjusted facility hours and extended online services.

Website: https://libguides.usu.edu/coronavirus

Sexual Assault and Anti-Violence Information Office

SAAVI advocates and support are available remotely to help survivors navigate resources and reporting options both on and off campus. Email saavi@usu.edu for more information.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/saavi/

Financial Aid Office

Learn about the potential impacts from COVID-19 on student financial aid.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/financialaid/resources/COVID19

Scholarship Office

Scholarship resources are available online. Email scholarships@usu.edu with scholarship questions.

Website: https://scholarships.usu.edu/

Disability Resource Center

Find information about potential accommodation resources, including for remote learning.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/drc/student/covid19-information

Office of Equity

All services to address sexual misconduct and discrimination in the USU campus community are being offered remotely. Please contact the office at 435-797-1266, carolyn.baker@usu.edu, or file an online report.

Website: https://equity.usu.edu/

USU Campus Recreation

From fitness at home, eSports Intramural Events, and nutrition coaching, Campus Recreation provides active, healthy remote services for all students.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/campusrec/

Center for Community Engagement

The center is gathering virtual volunteer opportunities from community partners. To provide service, contact the center.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/communityengagement/contact_us

Mental Health Resources

Many resources for mental wellness are available, including self-guided tools, crisis therapy, and counseling through telehealth. CAPS is also a daily adjusting to COVID-19 virtual discussion group.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/covid-19/staying-healthy/mental-wellness

The Student Health Center

USU Student Health is continuing to provide routine urgent care to students but are pre-screening for respiratory illness symptoms. Those with mild symptoms that may be related to COVID-19 may receive a consultation through telehealth. Please contact the center at 435-797-1660 to schedule an in-person or telehealth appointment.

Website: https://health.usu.edu/make-appointment

Student Affairs

If you are concerned about a fellow student, or have concerns for yourself, please reach out to a case manager in Student Affairs. , you may report it to the CARE Team through phone, email or video conference. Student conduct and student of concern services including the CARE Team, Behavioral Intervention Team (BIT), and the Student Affairs Case Manager are available through phone, email, or video conference.

Website: https://studentconduct.usu.edu/sid/

Student Involvement and Leadership Center

Stay connected through virtual USUSA events, fraternity and sorority life, club events, the HURD, and the Spirit Squad by following their social media accounts and participating in virtual events.

Website: https://involvement.usu.edu/

USU Inclusion Center

Support and advocacy are available for all students on a case-by-case basis. For updates on Inclusion Center virtual events, follow the Inclusion Center on Instagram and Facebook.

Website: https://inclusion.usu.edu/

Veterans Resource Office

The office is providing all services remotely, including assisting student veterans with payment of tuition and fees, GI Bill funding, deployment leave of absence, and support and guidance are available remotely.

Website: https://veterans.usu.edu/

Logan Campus Open-Access Computer Labs

Open-access labs remain open for students on the Logan campus with adjusted hours.

Website: https://it.usu.edu/labs/

Global Engagement

Global Engagement is continuing to provide remote and pre-approved in-person support for students and visiting scholars . Immigration and study abroad advisors are available for virtual appointments. Important immigration updates can be found on the ISSS Portal. Stay connected through virtual events through Instagram and Facebook. For more information or questions email global.engagement@usu.edu.

Website: globalengagement.usu.edu

Academic Support Programs

Academic Support Programs provides resources for students, faculty, parents of students, and others interested in discovering new ways to study effectively, learn efficiently, and achieve academically.

Website: https://www.usu.edu/asp/

Career Services

Career Services has designed a suite of virtual career resources. Students may also access their career coach to help achieve career goals. Visit the Virtual Career Center for additional support, virtual events/appointments and more.

Website: https://career-services.usu.edu/

CONTACT

Email: covid-19@usu.edu
