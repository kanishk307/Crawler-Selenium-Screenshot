Yes. The refund is pro-rated back to the week following spring break when students were asked to leave the residence halls.

When will we receive our room and board refund?

As soon as possible, within the next several weeks.

Will dining dollars be credited for the next semester?

No. Since dining is included in the cost of room and board for undergraduates, this is included in the refund that students will be receiving.

If I used a loan to pay for housing, will those funds be returned instead of getting a credit?

If you used a Federal Loan (Parent Plus, Graduate Plus, Subsidized or Unsubsidized loans) to pay towards the balance on your account, federal regulations require that a refund is given to the student and or parent. If you would prefer that we send the funds back to the loan servicer, please email Student Financial Services at sfs@bridgeport.edu as soon as possible to confirm.

If you used a private educational loan through a bank, those funds are treated as cash payment and will be issued as a credit towards future tuition, room and board.
