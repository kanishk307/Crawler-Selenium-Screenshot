What You Need To Know

Immunization records are due before moving into on-campus housing or by April 15th. Click here for more information.

New: Electronic Medical Records

We've made the switch to electronic medical records! Your provider may use a computer in the exam room or may "chart" in their office between appointments, providers can send Secure Messages with lab or test results or just to follow up after a visit and you can reply to these messages, too.
