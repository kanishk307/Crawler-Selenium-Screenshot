    • We've compiled a list of suggested playlists for various job duties and skills.

Supervisors

You have options for keeping your employees productive and engaged during this time, including working remotely. Tasks that can be done remotely include:

  • Performing research and benchmarking.
  • Reviewing and organizing files.
  • Developing or editing documents or reports.
  • Creating training or job aids.
  • Providing remote support to another area.

Read more about supervising remotely.
