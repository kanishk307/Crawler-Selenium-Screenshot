Status of Campus Buildings or Departments

Last updated: 6/18/20 10:10 a.m.

Facilities across Illinois State University have adjusted hours to comply with ongoing CDC guidelines and recommendations due to the Coronavirus (COVID-19). Illinois State is taking these measures to continue serving the campus community while taking decisive steps to promote health, safety, and security during this extraordinary circumstance.

Academic buildings on campus are open from 7 a.m.–5 p.m. Exceptions are noted below.

Refer to this page to view the latest on open/closed status of a number of campus buildings. 

Admissions

We are open 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m. with a minimal staff in Hovey Hall. The Atrium will remain closed.

Starting June 22, we will begin to receive registered visitors at the Bone Student Center in groups of 10 or fewer for Quad tours. These guests will not be brought into The Atrium unless inclement weather necessitates the presentation of a virtual tour indoors. In the event guests need to come inside the building, we will abide by social distancing and face covering guidelines.

Starting July 1, all Admissions spaces will be open and staffed. The plan for limited tours will continue until we progress into a new stage and under the direction of the Governor's office. Admissions staff members remain available to assist prospective students and parents with any questions during regular working hours at (309) 438-2181 or Admissions@IllinoisState.edu.

Alumni Center

The Alumni Center is open Monday–Friday from 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m.

Athletics

Redbird Arena, Kaufmann Football Building, and Horton Fieldhouse is open Monday–Friday from 7 a.m.-5 p.m.

Weibring Golf Club

In accordance with the Governor's executive order, the golf course at Weibring Golf Club is open for play with several strict guidelines in place to protect the health and safety of all patrons. View full guidelines and restrictions.

Graduate Admissions/Graduate School

Mail Services

Mail Services will still be receiving mail from the U.S. Postal Service throughout the stay-at-home period. This mail will be sorted and available for pick-up between 8–11 a.m. on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Mail Services will also be accepting outgoing mail. This mail will be processed; however, it is not guaranteed to go out the same day that we receive it.

Email Shannon Covey to check on your mail or to arrange to pick-up or drop off your mail. 

Regular mail delivery resumed June 15.

Marketplace at Linkins

Marketplace at Linkins will remain closed until further notice.

Metcalf and University High School

Operating under access control. Will remain closed until staff return.

Milner Library

Milner Library will be open for curbside delivery only beginning June 17. Other services will be added as processes become clarified. The building will open to patrons in August. For more information on library services, please contact Milner staff.

Registrar's Office

The main physical points of contact for the Office of the University Registrar in Moulton Hall such as the Registrar Service Center, Testing Services Office and the Veteran and Military Services Office are available Monday–Friday from 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m. Veterans and Military Services office will be closed on Fridays throughout the summer.

You can order a transcript online and it is recommended that you select the Electronic PDF delivery option to have the transcript sent directly to the recipient.

Please use the email and telephone contact information listed on our website to limit physical interaction. 

Research laboratories

Illinois State students who need access to research labs should contact their supervising faculty members for guidance.

Science Lab Building

Will remain key card access only until July 1.

Speech and Hearing Clinic

The Speech and Hearing Clinic is open for scheduled audiology appointments. 

Student Accounts Building

The Student Accounts Building is open Monday–Friday from 9 a.m.–4:30 p.m.

The Student Accounts and Collections departments are not open for walk ins, but are available via email. Any student with questions about their refund or account can reach out to stuaccts@illinoisstate.edu.

Student Fitness Center Recreation Facilities

The Student Fitness Center recreation facilities is closed to the public until further notice. Resources for those wishing to continue workouts at home are available at CampusRecreation.IllinoisState.edu.

Business offices only will be open Monday–Friday from 7:30 a.m.–5 p.m.

Student Health Services

Illinois State's Student Health Services will be open from 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. As an added precaution, all appointments must be made over the phone at (309) 438-2778 to allow for screening for potential symptoms before a patient comes to the office. Online appointment scheduling is not currently available. Students are being offered the option of having their visit by telephone if no physical exam is required to decrease exposure. Nurses are available by phone at (309) 438-7676 for questions and for arrangements to get prescription refills. The pharmacy and medical records office will remain open as usual.

University Galleries

Closed to the public until further notice. Online exhibitions and videos are available at Galleries.IllinoisState.edu.

University Housing Services

University Housing Services is open for walk-ins. Guests must wear a mask in the building. Zoom meetings are also available by request. Contact Housing@IllinoisState.edu for more information. 

Watterson Dining Commons

Watterson Dining Commons will remain open daily for the following hours of operation:

Breakfast: 6:30–8:30 a.m.
Lunch: 11 a.m.–1:30 p.m.
Dinner: 4:30–7 p.m.

Please note, all meals purchased will be for boxed meal, carry-out service only. If you need to make arrangements to pick up meals outside of scheduled meal periods, please call (309) 261-9378. If you have any dietary needs (food allergies, celiac disease, or other restrictions), please contact our Registered Dietitian, Desiray Yeaw, at dyeaw@ilstu.edu to ensure your needs are met.
