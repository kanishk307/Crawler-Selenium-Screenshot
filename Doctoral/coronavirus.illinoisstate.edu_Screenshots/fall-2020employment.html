Employment

Faculty and staff are the University's greatest resource. Supporting the success and safeguarding the well-being of all employees remains the highest priority and will be accomplished by doing the following.

  1. Provide training on health and safety practices and requirements related to COVID-19.
  2. Continue practices that reflect the most current guidance for individuals to request reasonable accommodations under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) by contacting the Office of Equal Opportunity and Access (OEOA) or request a leave of absence from Human Resources.
  3. Offer guidance to employees affected by COVID-19.
  4. Provide guidance to supervisors who have employees affected by COVID-19.
  5. Continue current practices related to the hiring process (testing, interviews, searches)
