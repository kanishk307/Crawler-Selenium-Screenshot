Student Updates

As we move forward in this unpredictable situation, we will continue working to keep all of your needs top of mind and to pivot as quickly, and as thoughtfully, as we can as new information becomes available.

  • Academics

    More than just moving classes to remote learning, our efforts to protect our students and community will temporarily impact the way you learn.

    Notable updates

    • Summer Term online - Mar 27, 2020
    • Classes will be delivered remotely - Mar 4, 2020
    • Pass/Fail Policy changes - Mar 30, 2020
    Academic updates
    • Classes
    • Advising
    • Commencement
    • Tutoring
    • Career Services
    • Technology
    • Textbooks

  • Campus Life

    The need for social distancing to reduce spread and ensure the health of our community has impacted most campus activities and use of UofL facilities.

    Notable updates

    • Campus housing changes - Mar 17, 2020
    • All events cancelled or postponed - Mar 17, 2020
    • Campus dining carryout only - Mar 17, 2020
    Campus updates
    • Housing
    • Dining
    • Events
    • Building Access
    • Fitness

  • Financial

    Transparency is vital to our collective well-being during these challenging times. UofL is committed to providing information about how these changes may financially impact both you and the university.

    Notable updates

    • Prorated housing credit - Mar 19, 2020
    • Prorated dining credit - Mar 19, 2020
    • Financial assistance - Mar 19, 2020
    Financial updates
    • Housing
    • Dining
    • Contacts
    • Assistance

  • Health

    Together we will persevere through this and come out the other side a stronger, more unified university community. To accomplish this we must focus on both the health of both ourselves and each other.

    Notable updates

    • Rec and fitness centers close - Mar 17, 2020
    • Mental health resources - Mar 12, 2020
    • Travel restrictions - Mar 12, 2020
