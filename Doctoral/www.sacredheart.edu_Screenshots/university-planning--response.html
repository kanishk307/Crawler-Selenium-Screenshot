University Planning & Response

In keeping with the standards set forth by the state of Connecticut and the CDC, SHU is currently planning for an on-campus start for Fall 2020.

Sacred Heart's Planning & Response

  • Students, faculty and staff will be tested for coronavirus before coming back to campus.
  • Students, faculty and staff will be asked to sign the Pioneer Promise upon returning to campus, pledging to follow safety guidelines to protect the health of everyone on campus.
  • Masks will be worn everywhere on campus except in students’ rooms, including classes, common areas and traveling between buildings.
  • Sanitizing wipes will be available outside of classrooms. Students and faculty must sanitize their area upon entering and leaving class.
  • Classes will be held in rooms at 50% capacity to allow for social distancing.
  • Most face-to-face classes will transfer to online after November 24.
  • Buildings will feature signage and one-way traffic flow to keep social distancing standards.
  • There will be accommodations made for students and faculty who have anxiety about returning to campus due to the virus.
  • Any student who refuses to wear a mask in class will be directed to leave and will receive an unexcused absence.
