COVID-19 Information and a Fall Return to Campus

See MCLA's Return to Work guide for all employees here.

MCLA intends to welcome students to campus and resume classes in person in September.

Read President Birges message regarding MCLAs return to campus plan here.

Although returning to campus this fall presents some risk, we will work to make the campus experience as safe as possible for everyone. Of course, this means we will have to significantly shift our way of learning and working. We are prepared to change this plan if confronted with new information or recommendations from the state.

MCLA has set up ahotline phone number for questions related to COVID-19 at 413-662-5550, between 8:30 a.m.-5 p.m., Monday-Friday.You can also leave us a message outside of these hours. Please use this number rather than calling individual offices.
