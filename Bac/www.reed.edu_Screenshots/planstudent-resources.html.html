Resources for Students & Family Members

Student Information Sessions

In July the college will host a series of virtual information sessions to discuss plans for fall 2020. Specific dates and information session topics will be announced soon.

Support Resources

The full spectrum of student support services—including tutoring, counseling services, peer advising, and disability accommodations—will be available during the fall semester, though many will operate in modified approaches in accordance with health and safety guidlines. Support for community members disproportionately affected by COVID-19 will be a key priority.

COVID-19 Financial Support for Students

Reed is committed to supporting students and families who are experiencing financial difficulty or incurring additional expenses as a result of the coronavirus pandemic. Students seeking financial support should contact the financial aid office.

Staff will work with students to determine whether their needs can be addressed through the financial aid process and may refer them to another campus resource.

Reed College Emergency Fund

This fund may be available for students who have urgent short-term or one-time financial needs. Students can apply online or contact Kat Buckspan in admission for more information.

Emergency Loans

Contact Sara Rosenberger in the business office for more information about loans for students who are experiencing financial hardship.

Student Work

Contact Kate Walford in the financial aid office for more information about student work.

Office of Financial Aid

For general queries regarding financial aid, students may email the financial aid office or schedule a virtual or phone meeting with staff.
