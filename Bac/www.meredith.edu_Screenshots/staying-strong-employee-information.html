» Staying Strong / Employee Information


This page features resources of particular interest for faculty and staff. More information will be added to this page as plans for 2020-21 are finalized.

Community Standards:

Guidance for employees, including requirements regarding face coverings, cleaning, and hygiene, are available on our Community Standards page.

What to Do if You are Sick or Exposed to COVID-19

Meredith employees are asked to self-monitor for COVID-19 symptoms. Please review guidance on what to do if you have symptoms or have been exposed to  COVID-19.

Human Resources Policies

Additional information is available on the HR page on MyMeredith (log in required)  

Staying Strong Signage

Meredith College has created a variety of signs for use on campus as part of the Staying Strong campaign to prevent the spread of COVID-19. Large format signs are available for order through the Copy Center starting July 6. All other campus signage is available for downloading to print on office printers, and/or for ordering through the Copy Center.  Details and downloadable templates are now available on the Marketing Department page on MyMeredith.
