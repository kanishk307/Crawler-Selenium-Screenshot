Important Summer/Fall 2020 Dates

  • July 9: Returning student deadline for submitting Commitment to Enroll Form (sent with email message July 2).
  • July 13-31. Room Draw for sophomores, juniors, and seniors.
  • Nov. 20: Last day of in-person instruction. Thanksgiving Break begins after last class.
  • Nov. 20-25: Move-out by appointment and details to be determined.
  • Nov. 30: All classes resume remotely.
  • Dec. 10: End of the semester. Exams will be conducted remotely (Dec. 13-18).
