Academic Continuity

View the pages in the Blended Learning section of the website for more information and details on academic continuity plans and support for faculty in the context of COVID-19.

Bryn Mawr College delivered all undergraduate, graduate, and postbaccalaureate courses online from through the remainder of the Spring 2020 semester.

Library & Information Technology Services has prepared a guide to help community members think through ways of teaching, learning, and collaborating remotely, and has added a page on additional library resources available.

Learn more about in-person summer research for undergraduates and graduate students.

Important Summer/Fall 2020 Dates

  • July 9: Returning student deadline for submitting Commitment to Enroll Form (sent with email message July 2).
  • July 13-31. Room Draw for sophomores, juniors, and seniors.
  • Nov. 20: Last day of in-person instruction. Thanksgiving Break begins after last class.
  • Nov. 20-25: Move-out by appointment and details to be determined.
  • Nov. 30: All classes resume remotely.
  • Dec. 10: End of the semester. Exams will be conducted remotely (Dec. 13-18).
