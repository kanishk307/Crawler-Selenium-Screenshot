DEPARTURE FROM CAMPUS

GROFF'S MOVING & STORAGE

Will be on campus parked in the Race Avenue parking lot 

behind Ware College House on the following dates:

  • Wednesday, 4/22 - 10 a.m. through 2 p.m.
  • Wednesday, 4/29 - 10 a.m. through 2 p.m.
  • Wednesday, 5/06 - 10 a.m. through 2 p.m.

 

Boxes and tape will be available in the Steinman College Center.

Carts will be available for use.

Please see the Shipping and Storage website HERE to print the forms needed to take your items to the truck for storage

Please let me know if you have any questions.
