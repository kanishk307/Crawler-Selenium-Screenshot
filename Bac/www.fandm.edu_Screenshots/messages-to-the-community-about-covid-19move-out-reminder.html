Move-Out Reminder

5/22/2020

Move-Out Reminder

Update - Reminder to Sign Up for Move Out Appointment    

Dear Students,

Thank you for your patience as the College awaited relevant guidance from the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Beginning May 18, on-campus housing, including residence halls, as well as special interest houses and theme houses, commenced a systematic, controlled, and scheduled-appointment move-out process for purposes of collecting your personal belongings. This process will continue through Tuesday, June 2. 

We understand this has been a difficult time, and we appreciate your consideration as we all work to manage the present circumstances. The College’s process will entail scheduled appointments and controlled, limited access to the College’s residential spaces so as to accommodate Pennsylvania’s social-distancing protocols and to provide the safest possible checkout process for our students returning to campus to collect belongings.

Please note that Lancaster County continues to be under a stay-at-home order in response to COVID-19 and is considered to be in the most restricted “Red Phase” of Pennsylvania’s reopening plan. Before coming to campus, please review the Commonwealth’s restrictions, as well as those of your home state or residence, with regard to travel, masks, social distancing and safety issues.  ttps://www.pa.gov/guides/responding-to-covid-19/

All students need to make arrangements for their belongings either by returning to collect them or having items packed and stored on their behalf. Any items left behind at the end of this process would be considered abandoned and discarded. If you have any questions, please contact moveout@fandm.edu.

Please read and carefully follow these instructions:

Signing up to move out

Please use the Sign-up Genius link below for your particular residence hall to select an available checkout appointment for specific day and time. Sign-up links are unique to each residential location. An F&M email address must be used in the sign-up process. 

Move Out Appointment Links:

    • Bonchek College House - South Ben Franklin

    • Brooks College House - Buchanan

    • Brooks College House- Marshall

    • New College House

    • Ware College House - North Ben Franklin

    • Weis College House

    • Schnader Hall 

    • Thomas Hall

    • Small Houses,Theme and Special Interest Houses
       

  • There will be three appointment times on weekdays: (8 - 10 a.m.), (10:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.) and (1 - 3  p.m.); and four times on weekends:  (8 - 10 a.m.), (10:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.), (1 - 3  p.m), and (3:30 - 5:30 p.m.)

  • A maximum of three (3) students per residence hall floor will be permitted during any given time slot. Special Interest and Theme Houses will be limited to one (1) student per time slot. Please coordinate with your roommate / suitemate to arrange to move out at different times.

  • Each student is permitted one helper to enter their building with them to assist in move-out.

  • A storage company will be available onsite some days. See Summer/Fall Storage below for information and dates.

  • Students are not permitted to stay overnight on campus for the move-out process. 

  • College staff will be checking email and are able to answer questions remotely but are not expected to have in-person contact with students during move-out.

Special Note: Retrieving items already packed and stored by the College

Students who have been notified that their rooms were professionally packed during the campus consolidation process will follow a different sign-up process. These students have received an individual email with instructions for their location and a link to sign up for a specific retrieval time.  

All students are required to make arrangements for their belongings, either by returning to collect them or having items packed and stored on their behalf. Any items left behind at the end of this process will be considered abandoned and discarded. If you have any questions about this, please contact moveout@fandm.edu

Prepare before you arrive

  • Anyone who has been sick or had known interaction with any individual with a confirmed or presumed case of COVID-19 should not come to campus until they have successfully completed a 14-day quarantine. 

  • Access to your residence hall or house is restricted to your assigned time.

  • Make sure to bring your room keys and F&M ID Card.

  • Please come prepared to depart with or store all of your items and bring all necessary boxes, tape, totes, etc. Storage on campus is NOT available. Consult local storage rental agents if you choose not to take everything home.

  • You and your helper must bring and wear a face mask throughout the move-out process. Wearing gloves is also encouraged.

  • Do not bring pets with you to the checkout.

  • Please bring your own carts and moving devices if possible.

  • There will be no elevator access in residence halls.

  • Please have patience with other students and their helpers as they move out of campus.

  • Practice social distancing and be safe.  

Parking guidelines

  • Driving directions and parking locations:
    https://www.fandm.edu/conferences-events/parking-for-conferences-events

  • Please park your car in the parking lot closest to your residence while you go into your room to organize and pack.

  • You may not drive or park on sidewalks or lawn areas.

  • Please do not move parking barricades. They have been placed to allow this process to work most smoothly for the majority of people.

 Summer / Fall Storage - Updated Times

  • Please consult local storage rental agents if you choose not to take everything home. Groff’s Storage and Moving will be on campus (behind Ware College House) some days during the moving out period. 

    • Saturday, May 23 - Monday, May 25 : No on-site service

    • Tuesday, May 26 - Friday, May 29 : 11 a.m. - 1 p.m.

    • Saturday, May 30 & Sunday, May 31:  No on-site service

Once you are on campus - moving out

Students are expected to do the following to complete the checkout process:

  • Access your residence using your F&M ID card. Only the main doors of residence halls will be unlocked to limit foot traffic and assist in social distancing.

  • Remove all belongings from your room.

  • Remove all trash from your room/suite and discard appropriately. Bulk trash and large items must be taken directly to the dumpster outside your residence hall.

  • Pull out all drawers to check for items in the back.

  • Check under the bed for forgotten items.

  • Close and lock your room window.

  • Turn off the lights.

  • Lock your room door and suite door.

  • Return your key at the key drop box nearest your residence.

  • Before you depart, login to inside.fandm.edu to complete the Express Checkout process in the Housing section.

  • In general: while on campus follow safe practices:

    • Wear a mask in hallways and common areas and when interacting with others. 

    • Keep six feet away. If you see friends from the community, remain 6 - 10 feet apart and avoid shaking hands, hugging, etc.

    • Keep your hands clean. Frequently wash your hands or use hand sanitizer – and make sure you dry them thoroughly – after touching doors/surfaces.   

Returning things on campus

  • Mailroom: The Steinman College Center mailroom is open from 11 a.m. - 3 p.m. daily so that you can pick up packages and mail.

  • Library: Books and other borrowed items may be dropped off at the book drop in front of the Shadek-Fackenthal at any time or at the drop off location in the lobby of the Steinman College Center between 11 a.m. - 3 p.m. daily.   

  • Athletics: For student-athletes who still have athletic equipment to return, please contact Robin Wert-Eller at robin.wert@fandm.edu to make arrangements to return your equipment while on campus.  In order to assist in staffing, when possible, please give us 48-72 hours notice prior to when you will be on campus.

  • Bookstore: The Bookstore on campus is closed; if you would like to return your books in person, an appointment must be made in advance via email. Rental books can be shipped back to the store using the free UPS label(s) available on the Bookstore site or returned at the beginning of the fall semester (before 9/4/20) without penalty

If you are unable to return to campus

We understand that it may not be possible for you to return to campus at this time due to extenuating circumstances. Please know that we are sensitive to your concerns and are flexible in our process for your return to campus to collect your belongings.  

Students who anticipate being unable to return to campus may arrange to have their room packed and stored here in Lancaster or packed and shipped to a location of their choice. This service is being offered by Groff’s Moving and Storage. Groff’s is a privately owned company close to campus. For ease of communication, the College is helping students connect to this service, but all arrangements, questions, and details will be managed directly with Groff’s.  

Important note: Because of the challenge of correctly identifying individual belongings in a double or triple room, all residents of a room must agree to have their items packed and stored together or wait to have their items removed until the others have returned for their belongings. 

Groff’s will coordinate access to residential rooms with the College after the student move-out process has concluded and provide College staff with a confirmation authorizing access to your room to pack your belongings. 

Questions about Groff’s services, process, and fees should be directed to:

Jenna Vuxta
jennavuxta@cegmovers.com

Questions: 

If you have questions about the process, please feel free to contact moveout@fandm.edu. A Student Affairs staff member will respond as soon as possible. 

We understand this is a highly stressful and uncertain time for everyone. Please be thoughtful toward others. Practice staying calm and patient while moving out.

Thank you and be safe!
