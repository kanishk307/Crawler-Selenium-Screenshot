Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund (HEERF) CARES Grant

You may qualify for an emergency grant funded by the United States CARES Act allotment of funds to the Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund (HEERF).  Most Alamance Community College (ACC) students are potentially eligible.

CARES/HEERF grants are for expenses related to the disruption of ACC campus operations (courses, services and facilities) in spring-summer 2020 due to Covid-19 responses.  Examples of potential expenses are on the CARES/HEERF request/certification form.

Grants are expected to range from $100 – $1,000, depending on a) what the student’s actual reported expenses are; b) how many applications are received, c) how many students have demonstrated financial need, and d) the availability of funds.

The priority deadline to request funds is May 25, 2020.  CARES/HEERF requests and FAFSAs received after May 25th will be processed weekly through June 30, 2020 as long as funds are available.

To obtain a CARES/HEERF grant you must:

  • Beginning May 11, submit a CARES/HEERF request/certification form that you will find in your Self Service E-forms. (Login instructions here)
    Note: Your username must be entered with “acc\” typed first.  The correct slash is the one between “backspace” key and “enter” key on a typical keyboard.  Your username entry should look like this:  acc\fmlast789
    Your password is the same as for Self Service, ACCess email, and Moodle.On that form, you will state your expenses due to Covid-19 campus disruptions at ACC.  No receipts or other documentation are required, but you will certify by your signature on the form that the stated expenses are correct.  And you must verify that your address is correct on your Self Service account.  If your address is incorrect, please contact Admissions at admissions@alamancecc.edu to update.
  • Submit a 2019-20 FAFSA (Free Application for Student Aid) if you have not done that already. If you need to submit a FAFSA you may visit FAFSA.gov or schedule an appointment for assistance at https://www.alamancecc.edu/financial-aid-site/financial-aid-workshops/.  Students do not need to demonstrate financial need to qualify for the CARES/HEERF grants—but they do need to have a FAFSA completed.

ACC staff will use your CARES/HEERF request/certification and your FAFSA to make sure you are eligible.  (Some students are not eligible, such as high school students, non-degree students, and international students.)  After your request/certification is approved, ACC will post the approval in your Self Service account.  Then the College will need about two weeks to process, prepare, and mail a check to the address you have on file with the College.

If you have any questions, please email financialaid@alamancecc.edu.
