2020 Summer Campus Safety Guidance

Dear ACC Summer Term Student:

We look forward to your return to ACC’s campus for one or more classes in the 2020 summer term.  Some of the services and spaces you have used in past semesters will be accessed differently this summer, due to coronavirus health considerations.

You will receive information from your instructor(s) about what to expect in your classes.  Parking lots are open.  When you arrive on campus, you should go directly to the building that houses your class(es).  Exterior doors are unlocked only for buildings that are in use for summer classes.  Visitors who are not enrolled for summer term, and those who have not pre-arranged a campus appointment, may not use campus facilities this summer.  For example, if you usually have a ride to ACC, and your driver usually waits on campus until the end of your class, the driver will not be permitted to wait in campus buildings this summer.

The College expects everyone to comply with 6-ft. social distancing, or if not feasible, then to wear a face mask during face-to-face interactions.  So, plan to bring your mask to campus every day, because you will need it from time to time.  If you do not have a face mask you may obtain one at the Main Building Information Desk, at the third floor entrance.  Please comply with social distancing when in hallways, elevators, stairways, and other circulation areas.  As long as supplies are available, the College will provide hand sanitizer in each classroom used for summer courses.

If you need help from campus services, or if you need to drop off forms or documents, then you should enter the Main Building on the third floor and ask for assistance at the Information Desk.

Many student services are available only remotely, and by advance appointment, this summer.  Administrative and service offices will not be open to walk-in traffic as the summer session begins. On-campus assistance will be phased in through the summer if feasible.

The College’s summer business hours are 7:30am – 5:30pm Monday – Thursday and 8am – 12pm Friday.  Most appointments will be held online or on the phone. A small number must be held on campus, face-to-face.
